Article IV,  Section 10.   [Oath of office.]
All officers made elective or appointive by this Constitution or by the laws
made in pursuance thereof, before entering upon the duties of their respective
offices, shall take and subscribe the following oath or affirmation: "I do
solemnly swear (or affirm) that I will support, obey and defend the
Constitution of the United States and the Constitution of this State, and that
I will discharge the duties of my office with fidelity.["]
