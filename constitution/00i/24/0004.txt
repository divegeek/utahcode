Article XXIV,  Section 4.   [Fines, penalties and forfeitures due the
territory -- Debts of the territory.]
All fines, penalties and forfeitures accruing to the people of the United
States in the Territory of Utah, shall inure to this State, and all debts,
liabilities and obligations of said Territory shall be valid against the
State, and enforced as may be provided by law.
