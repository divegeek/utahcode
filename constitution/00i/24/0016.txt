Article XXIV,  Section 16.   [When Constitution in force.]
The provisions of this Constitution shall be in force from the day on which
the President of the United States shall issue his proclamation, declaring the
State of Utah admitted into the Union; and the terms of all officers elected
at the first election under the provisions of this Constitution, shall
commence on the first Monday, next succeeding the issue of said proclamation.
Their terms of office shall expire when their successors are elected and
qualified under this Constitution.
Done in Convention at Salt Lake City, in the Territory of Utah, this eighth
day of May, in the year of our Lord one thousand eight hundred and ninety-
five, and of the Independence of the United States the one hundred and
nineteenth.
