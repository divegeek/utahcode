48-2c-1605.   Amended application for authority to transact business.

(1)  A foreign company authorized to transact business in this state shall deliver an amended application for authority to transact business to the division for filing if the foreign company changes:

(a)  its name or its assumed name;

(b)  the period of its duration; or

(c)  the state or country of its formation or organization.

(2)  The requirements of Section 48-2c-1604 for obtaining an original application for authority to transact business apply to filing an amended application for authority to transact business under this section.



Enacted by Chapter 260, 2001 General Session
