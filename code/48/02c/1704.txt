48-2c-1704.   Stay of proceedings.

Whether or not a demand for action was made, if the company promptly commences an investigation of the allegations made in the demand or complaint, the court may stay the action for such reasonable period as the court considers appropriate but, in any event, the stay shall expire at the earlier of 30 days or when the company's investigation is completed.



Enacted by Chapter 260, 2001 General Session
