48-1-45 (Repealed 07/01/13).   Name of registered limited liability partnership.

The name of a limited liability partnership shall contain the words "limited liability partnership" or the abbreviations "L.L.P." or "LLP" as the last words or letters of its name.



Enacted by Chapter 61, 1994 General Session 

Repealed by Chapter 353, 2011 General Session
