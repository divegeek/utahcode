48-1-3.1 (Repealed 07/01/13).   Joint venture defined -- Application of chapter.

(1)  A joint venture is an association of two or more persons to carry on as co-owners of a single business enterprise.

(2)  This chapter governs the property and transfer rights of joint ventures.
