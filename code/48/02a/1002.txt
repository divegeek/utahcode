48-2a-1002.   Proper plaintiff.
In a derivative action, the plaintiff must be a partner at the time of
bringing the action and:
(1)  must have been a partner at the time of the transaction of which he
complains; or
(2)  his status as a partner must have devolved upon him by operation of law
or pursuant to the terms of the partnership agreement from a person who was a
partner at the time of the transaction.
