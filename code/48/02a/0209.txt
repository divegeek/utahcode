48-2a-209.   Delivery of certificates to limited partners.

Upon the return by the division pursuant to Section 48-2a-206 of a stamped copy of any certificate, the general partners shall promptly deliver or mail a copy of the certificate of limited partnership and each certificate of amendment or cancellation to each limited partner unless the partnership agreement provides otherwise.
