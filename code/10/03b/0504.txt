10-3b-504.   Limitations on adoption of a resolution and filing of a
petition.
A resolution may not be adopted under Subsection 10-3b-503(1)(a) and a
petition may not be filed under Subsection 10-3b-503(1)(b) within:
(1)  two years after an election at which voters reject a proposal to change
the municipality's form of government, if the resolution or petition proposes
changing to the same form of government that voters rejected at the election;
or
(2)  four years after the effective date of a change in the form of municipal
government.
