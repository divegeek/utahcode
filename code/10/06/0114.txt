10-6-114.   Budget -- Public hearing on tentatively adopted budget.
At the time and place advertised, or at any time and place to which the public
hearing may be adjourned, the governing body shall hold a public hearing on
the budgets tentatively adopted. All interested persons in attendance shall be
given an opportunity to be heard, for or against, the estimates of revenue and
expenditures or any item thereof in the tentative budget of any fund.
