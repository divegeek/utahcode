10-6-145.   City treasurer -- Special assessments -- Application of
proceeds.
All money received by the treasurer on any special assessment shall be applied
to the payment of the improvement for which the assessment was made. The money
shall be used for the payment of interest and principal on bonds or other
indebtedness issued in settlement thereof, and shall be used for no other
purpose whatever, except as otherwise provided in Section 10-6-131.
