10-6-143.   City treasurer or deputy -- Duties with respect to issuance of
checks.
The treasurer, or in his absence a deputy treasurer appointed by the governing
body, shall sign all checks prepared by the auditor or recorder. Prior to
affixing the signature, the treasurer or deputy treasurer shall determine that
a sufficient amount is on deposit in the appropriate bank account of the city
to honor the check. The governing body may also designate a person, other than
the city auditor or the city recorder, to countersign checks.
