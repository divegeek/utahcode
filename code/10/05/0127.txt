10-5-127.   Signing of checks -- Determination of sufficiency of account.
The town treasurer, or in his absence a deputy treasurer appointed by the
council, shall sign all checks prepared by the town clerk. Prior to affixing
the signature, the treasurer or deputy treasurer shall determine that a
sufficient amount is on deposit in the appropriate bank account of the town to
honor the check. The council may also designate any town officer to
countersign checks.
