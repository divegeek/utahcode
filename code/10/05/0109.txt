10-5-109.   Adoption of budgets -- Filing.
Prior to June 22 of each year, or August 17 in the case of a property tax
increase under Sections 59-2-919 through 59-2-923, the council shall by
resolution or ordinance adopt a budget for the ensuing fiscal year for each
fund for which a budget is required under this chapter.  A copy of the final
budget for each fund shall be filed with the state auditor within 30 days
after adoption.
