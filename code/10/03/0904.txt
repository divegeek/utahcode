10-3-904.   Books and supplies -- Recording, filing and inspection.
The city engineer's office shall be supplied with all necessary books, cases
and supplies for recording and filing as required. The city engineer shall
record and file all drawings and documents pertaining to public lands and
improvements. Those made in his office shall be placed on record as soon as
completed and shall then be open for public inspections, and any person
copying the same or taking notes therefrom may do so in pencil only. He shall
keep the records and files in good condition and turn the same over to his
successor in office. He shall allow no alteration, mutilation or changes to be
made in any matter of record, and shall be held strictly accountable for the
same.
