10-3-1312.   Violation of disclosure requirements -- Penalties -- Rescission of prohibited transaction.

If any transaction is entered into in connection with a violation of Section 10-3-1305, 10-3-1306, 10-3-1307, or 10-3-1308, the municipality:

(1)  shall dismiss or remove the appointed or elected officer or municipal employee who knowingly and intentionally violates this part from employment or office; and

(2)  may rescind or void any contract or subcontract entered into pursuant to that transaction without returning any part of the consideration received by the municipality.
