10-3-710.   Publication in book, pamphlet or looseleaf form -- State statutes.

Ordinances revised, codified, compiled and published in book, pamphlet or looseleaf form by authority of the governing body need not be printed or published in any other manner, except that the ordinance adopting the revision, codification or compilation shall be published or posted in the manner provided by law. Provisions of state law may be adopted by reference. Any changes necessary to conform those state laws with municipal ordinance shall be noted.



Enacted by Chapter 48, 1977 General Session
