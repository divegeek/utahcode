10-2-601.   Consolidation of two or more municipalities.
The process for consolidating municipalities shall begin by filing with the
county legislative bodies of the respective counties in which the
municipalities are located:
(1)  resolutions passed by the governing bodies of the municipalities which
state their intention and desire to form a consolidated municipality; or
(2)  petitions signed by at least 10% of the registered voters in each of the
municipalities to be included with the boundaries of the consolidated
municipality.
