10-2-612.   New municipality -- Ownership of property -- Indebtedness of
original municipalities.
Any consolidated municipality shall be deemed to be a continuation of the
merged municipalities, except as herein expressly provided, and shall own all
of the assets, property, records, seals, equipment, and be responsible for the
liabilities of each and all of the municipalities dissolved by the
consolidation. The new municipality shall require the inhabitants of an
original municipality included in the consolidation, by special tax levy, to
satisfy any indebtedness incurred by the original municipalities provided
inhabitants residing in other parts of the consolidated municipality did not
or do not benefit by the revenue or services obtained by the expenditures
causing the indebtedness. The governing body of the consolidated municipality
shall be subject to the terms of the consolidation plan.
