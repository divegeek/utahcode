10-2-408.   Denying or approving the annexation petition -- Notice of
approval.
(1)  After receipt of the commission's decision on a protest under Subsection
10-2-416(2), a municipal legislative body may:
(a)  deny the annexation petition; or
(b)  if the commission approves the annexation, approve the annexation
petition consistent with the commission's decision.
(2)  Within 10 days after approving an annexation under Subsection (1)(b) of
an area that is partly or entirely within a township, the municipal
legislative body shall send notice of the approval to the legislative body of
the county in which the township is located.
