10-1-408.   Procedure for taxes erroneously recovered from customers.
A customer may not bring a cause of action against a telecommunications
provider on the basis that the telecommunications provider erroneously
recovered from the customer municipal telecommunications license taxes
authorized by this part unless the customer meets the same requirements that a
purchaser is required to meet to bring a cause of action against a seller for
a refund or credit as provided in Subsection 59-12-110.1(3).
