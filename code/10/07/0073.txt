10-7-73.   Corporate violation -- Hearing -- Penalty imposed to be a fine.

After the plea of the corporation is entered the court shall fix a time for the hearing of the cause, and thereafter the proceedings therein shall be the same as in the cases of natural persons charged with violating a city or town ordinance, except that in cases of conviction the penalty imposed in all instances shall be by way of fine.



Amended by Chapter 378, 2010 General Session
