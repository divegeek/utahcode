10-7-72.   Appearance by agent of corporation -- Bench warrant for default.

At the time appointed in the summons, the corporation shall appear by agent or attorney and plead thereto the same as a natural person.  In case no appearance is made on or before the hour appointed, the court may issue a bench warrant for the person served as the officer or agent of the corporation, requiring him to be brought forthwith before the court to plead on its behalf.
