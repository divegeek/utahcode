10-7-12.   Scarcity of water -- Limitation on use.
In the event of scarcity of water the mayor of any city or the president of
the board of trustees of any town may, by proclamation, limit the use of water
for any purpose other than domestic purposes to such extent as may be required
for the public good in the judgment of the board of commissioners or city
council of any city or the board of trustees of any town.
