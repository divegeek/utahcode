61-2d-107.   Prohibition on single premium credit life insurance.
A borrower credit transaction involving a high-cost mortgage may not include:
(1)  the offer or sale of any insurance policy, on a single premium or single
fee basis, that insures, guarantees, or indemnifies the repayment of the
outstanding balance of the loan against:
(a)  death;
(b)  illness;
(c)  accident;
(d)  disability; or
(e)  unemployment of the borrower; or
(2)  debt cancellation or a debt suspension agreement.
