61-1-18.   Division of Securities established -- Director -- Investigators.
(1) (a)  There is established within the Department of Commerce a Division of
Securities.
(b)  The division is under the direction and control of a director.  The
executive director shall appoint the director with the governor's approval.
(c)  Subject to Section 61-1-18.5, the division shall administer and enforce
this chapter.
(d)  The director shall hold office at the pleasure of the governor.
(2)  The director, with the approval of the executive director, may employ the
staff necessary to discharge the duties of the division or commission at
salaries to be fixed by the director according to standards established by the
Department of Human Resource Management.
(3)  An investigator employed pursuant to Subsection (2) who meets the
training requirements of Subsection 53-13-105(3) may be designated a special
function officer, as defined in Section 53-13-105, by the director, but is not
eligible for retirement benefits under the Public Safety Employee's Retirement
System.
