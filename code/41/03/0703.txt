41-3-703.   Violations as felonies.
(1)  A person may not forge, falsify, or counterfeit any license, special
plate, temporary permit, in-transit permit, decal, or other document issued by
the division or any other state or jurisdiction.
(2)  A person may not hold or use any license, special plate, temporary
permit, in-transit permit, decal, or other document issued by the division or
any other state or jurisdiction knowing it to have been forged, falsified, or
counterfeited.
(3)  A violation of Subsection (1) or (2) is a third degree felony.
