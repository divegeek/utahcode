41-3-802.   Definitions.
As used in this part:
(1) (a)  "Consignee" means a dealer who accepts vehicles for sale under an
agreement that the dealer will pay the consignor for any sold vehicle and will
return any unsold vehicles.
(b)  "Consignee" does not include a wholesale motor vehicle auction.
(2)  "Consignor" means a person who places a vehicle with a consignee for
consignment sale.
