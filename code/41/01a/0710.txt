41-1a-710.   Certificate of origin required for acquisition or resale of
vehicle, vessel, or outboard motor.
(1)  A dealer may not acquire a new  vehicle, vessel, or outboard motor
without obtaining a manufacturer's or importer's certificate of origin.
(2)  A manufacturer, importer, dealer, or other person may not sell or
otherwise dispose of a vehicle, vessel, or outboard motor for purposes of
resale without delivering a manufacturer's or importer's certificate of origin
to the purchaser or the new owner.
(3)  The division may prescribe uniform standards for the size and content of
certificates of origin.
