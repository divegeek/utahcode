41-1a-709.   Dealer transfer of used off-highway vehicle, vessel, or
outboard motor.
Upon the resale or subsequent transfer by a dealer of a used off-highway
vehicle, vessel, or outboard motor, the dealer shall endorse the certificate
of title and forward it, accompanied by the transferee's application for a
certificate of title, or if desired by the purchaser, and as applicable, an
affidavit of Mobile Home Affixture, to the division.
