41-1a-1309.   Boarding with intent to commit injury to motor vehicle, trailer, or semitrailer -- Class C misdemeanor.

It is a class C misdemeanor for a person with intent to commit any criminal mischief, injury, or other crime to:

(1)  climb into or upon a motor vehicle, trailer, or semitrailer, whether it is in motion or at rest;

(2)  attempt to manipulate any of the levers, starting mechanism, brakes, or other mechanism or device of a motor vehicle, trailer, or semitrailer while the same is at rest and unattended; or

(3)  set in motion any motor vehicle, trailer, or semitrailer while the same is at rest and unattended.
