41-1a-214.   Registration card to be signed, carried, and exhibited.
(1)  A registration card shall be signed by the owner in ink in the space
provided.
(2)  A registration card shall be carried at all times in the vehicle to which
it was issued.
(3)  The person driving or in control of a vehicle shall display the
registration card upon demand of a peace officer or any officer or employee of
the division.
