41-1a-1303.   Driving without registration or certificate of title -- Class C misdemeanor.

Except as provided in Section 41-1a-211 or 41-1a-1303.5, a person may not drive or move, or an owner may not knowingly permit to be driven or moved upon any highway any vehicle of a type required to be registered in this state:

(1)  that is not properly registered or for which a certificate of title has not been issued or applied for; or

(2)  for which the required fee has not been paid.



Amended by Chapter 245, 2013 General Session
