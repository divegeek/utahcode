41-1a-118.   Seizure of documents and plates -- Grounds -- Receipt.
(1)  The division and peace officers may take possession of any certificate of
title, registration card, registration decal, permit, license  plate, or any
other article issued by the division:
(a)  upon expiration, suspension, revocation, alteration, or cancellation of
it;
(b)  that is fictitious;
(c)  that has been unlawfully or erroneously issued; or
(d)  that is unlawfully or erroneously displayed.
(2)  A receipt shall be issued for any confiscated item.
