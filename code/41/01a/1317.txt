41-1a-1317.   Selling or buying without identification numbers -- Penalty.
It is a second degree felony for a person to knowingly buy, receive, dispose
of, sell, offer for sale, or have in his possession any motor vehicle,
trailer, semitrailer, or engine removed from a motor vehicle, from which the
identification number has been removed, defaced, covered, altered, or
destroyed for the purpose of concealing or misrepresenting the identity of the
motor vehicle or engine.
