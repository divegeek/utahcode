41-1a-804.   Garagemen, repair shops, and service stations -- Duty to report
number violations.
A person owning, conducting, managing, or operating a service station, marina,
marine dealership, public garage, paint shop, or repair shop for vehicles,
vessels, or outboard motors shall immediately notify the local peace officers
of any vehicle, vessel, or outboard motor that has any identification number
that has apparently been altered, obliterated, or removed.
