41-1a-1205.   Disposition of driver education tax -- Expense appropriation.
(1)  The automobile driver education tax collected under Section 41-1a-1204
shall be placed to the credit of the Automobile Driver Education Tax Account
within the Uniform School Fund.
(2)  The necessary expenses of the commission incurred in the administration
and collection of the tax shall be paid from its legislative appropriation in
the General Fund, which fund shall be reimbursed by a transfer for the
expenses from the legislative appropriation of the Uniform School Fund.
