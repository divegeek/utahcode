41-6a-708.   Signs and markings on roadway -- No-passing zones --
Exceptions.
(1) (a)  A highway authority may designate no-passing zones on any portion of
a highway under its jurisdiction if the highway authority determines passing
is especially hazardous.
(b)  A highway authority shall designate a no-passing zone under Subsection
(1)(a) by placing appropriate traffic-control devices on the highway.
(2) A person operating a vehicle may not drive on the left side of:
(a)  the roadway within the no-passing zone; or
(b)  any pavement striping designed to mark the no-passing zone.
(3)  Subsection (2) does not apply:
(a)  under the conditions described under Subsection 41-6a-701(1)(b); or
(b)  to a person operating a vehicle turning left onto or from an alley,
private road, or driveway.
