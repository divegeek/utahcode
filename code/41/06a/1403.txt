41-6a-1403.   Motor vehicle left unattended -- Requirements.
(1)  A person operating or in charge of a motor vehicle may not permit the
vehicle to stand unattended without:
(a)  stopping the engine;
(b)  locking the ignition and removing the key;
(c)  placing the transmission in "park" or the gears in "low" or "reverse" if
the vehicle has a manual shift; or
(d)  effectively setting the brakes thereon.
(2)  A person shall turn  the front wheels to the curb or side of the highway
when standing a vehicle on any perceptible grade.
