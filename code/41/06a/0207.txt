41-6a-207.   Uniform application of chapter -- Effect of local ordinances.
(1)  The provisions of this chapter are applicable throughout this state and
in all of its political subdivisions and municipalities.
(2)  A local highway authority may not enact or enforce any rule or ordinance
in conflict with the provisions of this chapter.
(3)  A local highway authority may adopt:
(a)  ordinances consistent with this chapter; and
(b)  additional traffic ordinances not in conflict with this chapter.
