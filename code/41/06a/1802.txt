41-6a-1802.   Definitions.
As used in this part:
(1)  "Child restraint device" means a child restraint device that meets
standards adopted under Section 41-6a-1601.
(2)  "Motor vehicle" means a vehicle defined in Section 41-1a-102, except
vehicles that are not equipped with safety belts by the manufacturer.
(3)  "Safety belt" means a safety belt or seat belt system that meets
standards adopted under Section 41-6a-1601.
(4)  "Seating position" means any area within the passenger compartment of a
motor vehicle in which the manufacturer has installed a safety belt.
