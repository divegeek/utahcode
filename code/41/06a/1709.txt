41-6a-1709.   Coasting prohibited.

(1)  The operator of a motor vehicle, when traveling on a downgrade, may not coast with the gears or transmission of the vehicle in neutral.

(2)  The operator of a truck or bus, when traveling on a downgrade, may not coast with the clutch disengaged.
