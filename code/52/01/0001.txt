52-1-1.   Official bonds to run to state, county, city or other agency.
When the law directs that a public officer shall give a bond without
prescribing to whom it shall run it shall be made, if he is a state officer,
to the state; if a county, precinct or district officer, to the county; if a
municipal officer, to the city or town; and if a school officer, to the board
of education.
