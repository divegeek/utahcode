52-7-201.   Reimbursement of attorney fees and court costs in Article V
lawsuits.
If any public officer is named as a defendant in a civil suit that alleges a
violation of Article V of the Utah Constitution, and that lawsuit is dismissed
or results in a judgment in favor of the defendant, the public officer may, by
complying with the requirements and procedures of this chapter, submit a claim
to the state for reimbursement of the defendant’s reasonable attorney fees
and court costs necessarily incurred in the defense of that civil suit.
