63J-1-218.   Reduction in federal funds -- Agencies to reduce budgets.
(1)  In any fiscal year in which federal grants to be received by state
agencies, departments, divisions, or institutions are reduced below the level
estimated in the appropriations acts for that year, the programs supported by
those grants must be reduced commensurate with the amount of the federal
reduction unless the Legislature appropriates state funds to offset the loss
in federal funding.
(2)  This program modification shall be reported to the Legislature through
the Executive Appropriations Committee and the Office of the Legislative
Fiscal Analyst.
