63J-1-214.   Warrants -- Not to be drawn until claim processed --
Redemption.
(1)  No warrant to cover any claim against any appropriation or fund shall be
drawn until such claim has been processed as provided by law.
(2)  The state treasurer shall return all redeemed warrants to the state
fiscal officer for purposes of reconciliation, post-audit and verification of
the state treasurer's fund balances.
