63J-4a-203.   Memorandum of understanding with fraud unit.

The inspector general shall enter into a memorandum of understanding with the fraud unit to:

(1)  formalize communication, cooperation, coordination of efforts, and the sharing of information, on a regular basis, between the office and the fraud unit;

(2)  provide for reporting criminal activity discovered by the office to the fraud unit;

(3)  ensure that investigations and other actions by the office and the fraud unit do not conflict; and

(4)  provide for the sharing and classification of records between the office and the fraud unit under the Government Records Access and Management Act.
