62A-5-315.   Petition for reexamination.
(1)  A resident committed under Section 62A-5-312, or his parent, spouse,
legal guardian, relative, or attorney, may file a petition for reexamination
with the district court of the county in which the resident is domiciled or
detained.
(2)  Upon receipt of that petition, the court shall conduct proceedings under
Section 62A-5-312.
