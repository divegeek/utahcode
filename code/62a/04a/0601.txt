62A-4a-601.   Definitions.

For purposes of this part:

(1)  "Child placing" means:

(a)  receiving, accepting, or providing custody or care for a child, temporarily or permanently, for the purpose of finding a person to adopt the child; or

(b)  placing a child, temporarily or permanently, in a home for adoption or substitute care.

(2)  "Child placing agency" means an individual, agency, firm, corporation, association, or group children's home that engages in child placing.
