62A-4a-705.   Fulfillment of requirements.

Requirements for visitation, inspection, or supervision of children, homes, institutions, or other agencies in another party state which may apply under Part 2 of this chapter shall be deemed to be met if performed pursuant to an agreement entered into by appropriate officers or agencies of this state, or a subdivision thereof, as contemplated by Paragraph (2) of Article V of the Interstate Compact on the Placement of Children.
