62A-4a-605.   Establishing proof of authority.
A child placing agency is not required to present its license, issued under
Chapter 2 of this title, or its certificate of incorporation, or proof of its
authority to consent to adoption, as proof of its authority in any proceeding
in which it is an interested party, unless the court or a party to the
proceeding requests that the agency or its representative establish proof of
authority.
