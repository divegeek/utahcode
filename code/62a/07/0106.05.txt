62A-7-106.5.   Annual review of programs and facilities.
(1) (a) The division shall annually review all programs and facilities that
provide services to juveniles who have committed a delinquent act, in this
state or in any other state, which would constitute a felony or misdemeanor if
committed by an adult, and license those programs and facilities that are in
compliance with standards approved by the board.  The division shall provide
written reviews to the managers of those programs and facilities.
(b)  Based upon policies established by the board, programs or facilities that
are unable or unwilling to comply with the approved standards may not be
licensed.
(2)  Any private facility or program providing services under this chapter
that willfully fails to comply with the standards established by the division
is guilty of a class B misdemeanor.
