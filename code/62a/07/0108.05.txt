62A-7-108.5.   Records -- Property of division.
(1)  All records maintained by programs that are under contract with the
division to provide services to youth offenders, are the property of the
division and shall be returned to it when the youth offender is terminated
from the program.
(2)  The division shall maintain an accurate audit trail of information
provided to other programs or agencies regarding youth offenders under its
jurisdiction.
