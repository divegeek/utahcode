62A-7-107.5.   Contracts with private providers.
(1)  This chapter does not prohibit the division from contracting with private
providers or other agencies for the construction, operation, and maintenance
of juvenile facilities or the provision of care, treatment, and supervision of
youth offenders who have been committed to the care of the division.
(2)  All programs for the care, treatment, and supervision of youth offenders
committed to the division shall be licensed in compliance with division
standards within six months after commencing operation.
