19-6-207.   Facility at site approved in siting plan -- Exemption from
zoning and local approval requirements -- Transportation restrictions limited.
(1)  The construction or operation of a hazardous waste treatment, storage, or
disposal facility at a site included within the siting plan is not required to
conform to any local zoning or other land use regulation, law, or ordinance.
(2)  The owner of any hazardous waste treatment, storage, or disposal facility
proposed to be located at a site included in the siting plan is not required
to obtain approval of the site from any county or municipal planning
commission or similar authority and no local unit of government may prohibit
or unduly restrict the transportation of hazardous waste through the
governmental unit to an approved hazardous waste treatment, storage, or
disposal facility.
