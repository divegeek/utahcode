19-1-305.   Administrative enforcement proceedings -- Tolling of limitation
period.
Issuing a notice of a violation, an order, or a notice of agency action under
this title tolls the running of the period of limitation for commencing a
civil action to assess or collect a penalty until the sooner of:
(1)  the day on which the notice of violation, order, or agency action becomes
final under Title 63G, Chapter 4, Administrative Procedures Act; or
(2)  three years from the day on which the department issues a notice or order
described in this section.
