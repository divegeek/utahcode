40-6-7.   Agreements for repressuring or pressure maintenance or cycling or
recycling operations -- Plan for development and operation of pool or field.
(1)  An agreement for repressuring or pressure maintenance operations, cycling
or recycling operations, including the extraction and separation of liquid
hydrocarbons from natural gas, or for carrying on any other methods of unit or
cooperative development or operation of a field or pool or a part of either,
is authorized and may be performed, and shall not be held or construed to
violate any statutes relating to trusts, monopolies, or contracts and
combinations in restraint of trade, if the agreement is approved by the board
as being in the public interest and promotes conservation, increases ultimate
recovery and prevents waste of oil or gas provided the agreement protects the
correlative rights of each owner or producer.
(2)  A plan for the development and operation of a pool or field shall be
presented to the board and may be approved after notice and hearing.
