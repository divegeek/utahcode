78B-2-215.   Adverse possession -- Payment of taxes -- Proviso -- Tax title.

Payment of all the taxes levied and assessed upon the real property for a period of not less than four years by the holder of a tax title to the real property or his predecessors is sufficient to satisfy the requirements of this chapter regarding the payment of taxes necessary to establish adverse possession.
