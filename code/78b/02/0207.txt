78B-2-207.   Actions or defenses founded upon title to real estate.
An action, defense, or counterclaim to an action based upon title to the
property or entitlement to the rents or profits from the property shall be
brought:
(1)  not later than seven years after the act on which it is based; and
(2)  by the ancestor, predecessor, or grantor of the person who owned or
possessed the property for seven years before the act in Subsection (1) took
place.
