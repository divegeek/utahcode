78B-15-601.   Proceeding authorized -- Definition.
(1)  An adjudicative proceeding may be maintained to determine the parentage
of a child.  A judicial proceeding is governed by the rules of civil
procedure.  An administrative proceeding is governed by Title 63G, Chapter 4,
Administrative Procedures Act.
(2)  For the purposes of this part, "divorce" also includes an annulment.
