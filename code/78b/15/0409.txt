78B-15-409.   Search of records -- Certificate.
(1)  Upon the request of an individual, tribunal, or agency identified in
Section 78B-15-405, the Office of Vital Records shall search its records for
any registration made pursuant to Section 78B-15-401 and furnish to the
requestor a certificate of search which shall be signed on behalf of the
office and state that:
(a)  a search has been made of the records of the Office of Vital Records; and
(b)  a registration containing the information required to identify the
registrant:
(i)  has been found and is attached to the certificate of search; or
(ii)  has not been found.
(2)  A petitioner shall file the certificate of search with the tribunal in
connection with a proceeding for adoption.
