78B-15-706.   Effect of dissolution of marriage.
(1)  If a marriage is dissolved before placement of eggs, sperm, or an embryo,
the former spouse is not a parent of the resulting child unless the former
spouse consented in a record that if assisted reproduction were to occur after
a divorce, the former spouse would be a parent of the child.
(2)  The consent of the former spouse to assisted reproduction may be revoked
by that individual in a record at any time before placement of eggs, sperm, or
embryos.
