78B-15-103.   Scope -- Choice of law.
(1)  This chapter applies to determinations of parentage in this state.
(2)  The tribunal shall apply the law of this state to adjudicate the parent-
child relationship.  The applicable law may not depend upon:
(a)  the place of birth of the child; or
(b)  the past or present residence of the child.
(3)  This chapter may not create, enlarge, or diminish parental rights or
duties under other laws of this state.
