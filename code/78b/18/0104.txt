78B-18-104.   Validity of unsworn declaration.

(1)  Except as otherwise provided in Subsection (2), if a law of this state requires or permits use of a sworn declaration, an unsworn declaration meeting the requirements of this chapter has the same effect as a sworn declaration.

(2)  This chapter does not apply to:

(a)  depositions;

(b)  oaths of office;

(c)  oaths required to be given before a specified official other than a notary public;

(d)  declarations to be recorded pursuant to Title 57, Real Estate; or

(e)  oaths required by Section 75-2-504.



Enacted by Chapter 100, 2009 General Session
