78B-14-612.   Recognition of order modified in another state.
If a child-support order issued by a tribunal of this state is modified by a
tribunal of another state which assumed jurisdiction pursuant to this chapter,
a tribunal of this state:
(1)  may enforce its order that was modified only as to arrears and interest
accruing before the modification;
(2)  may provide appropriate relief for violations of its order which occurred
before the effective date of the modification; and
(3)  shall recognize the modifying order of the other state, upon
registration, for the purpose of enforcement.
