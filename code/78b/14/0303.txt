78B-14-303.   Application of law of state.
Except as otherwise provided in this chapter, a responding tribunal of this
state shall:
(1)  apply the procedural and substantive law generally applicable to similar
proceedings originating in this state and may exercise all powers and provide
all remedies available in those proceedings; and
(2)  determine the duty of support and the amount payable in accordance with
the law and support guidelines of this state.
