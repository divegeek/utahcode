78B-14-317 (Superseded 07/01/15).   Communications between tribunals.

A tribunal of this state may communicate with a tribunal of another state or foreign country or political subdivision in a record, or by telephone or other means, to obtain information concerning the laws, the legal effect of a judgment, decree, or order of that tribunal, and the status of a proceeding in the other state or a foreign country or political subdivision.  A tribunal of this state may furnish similar information by similar means to a tribunal of another state or foreign country or political subdivision.
