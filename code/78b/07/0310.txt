78B-7-310.   Transitional provision.
This part applies to protection orders issued before July 1, 2006 and to
continuing actions for enforcement of foreign protection orders commenced
before July 1, 2006.  A request for enforcement of a foreign protection order
made on or after July 1, 2006 for violations of a foreign protection order
occurring before July 1, 2006 is governed by this part.
