78B-11-103.   Notice.
(1)  Except as otherwise provided in this chapter, a person gives notice to
another person by taking action that is reasonably necessary to inform the
other person in ordinary course, whether or not the other person acquires
knowledge of the notice.
(2)  A person has notice if the person has knowledge of the notice or has
received notice.
(3)  A person receives notice when it comes to the person's attention or the
notice is delivered at the person's place of residence or place of business,
or at another location held out by the person as a place of delivery of such
communications.
