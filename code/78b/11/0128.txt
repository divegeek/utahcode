78B-11-128.   Venue.
A motion pursuant to Section 78B-11-106 must be made in the court of the
county in which the agreement to arbitrate specifies the arbitration hearing
is to be held or, if the hearing has been held, in the court of the county in
which it was held.  Otherwise, the motion may be made in the court of any
county in which an adverse party resides or has a place of business or, if no
adverse party has a residence or place of business in this state, in the court
of any county in this state.  All subsequent motions must be made in the court
hearing the initial motion unless the court otherwise directs.
