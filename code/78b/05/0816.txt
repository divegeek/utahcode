78B-5-816.   Right of rescission or disavowal of release, settlement, or statement by injured person in addition to other provisions.

The rights provided by Sections 78B-5-812 through 78B-5-816 are intended to be in addition to, and not in lieu of, any rights of rescission, rules of evidence, or provisions otherwise existing in the law.
