78B-5-705.   Unsworn declaration in lieu of affidavit.
(1)  If the Utah Rules of Criminal Procedure, Civil Procedure, or Evidence
require or permit a written declaration upon oath, an individual may, with
like force and effect, provide an unsworn written declaration, subscribed and
dated under penalty of this section, in substantially the following form:
"I declare (or certify, verify, or state) under criminal penalty of the State
of Utah that the foregoing is true and correct.
Executed on (date).
(Signature)".
(2)  A person who knowingly makes a false written statement as provided under
Subsection (1) is guilty of a class B misdemeanor.
