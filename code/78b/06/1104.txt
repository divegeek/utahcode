78B-6-1104.   Agricultural operations -- Nuisance liability.

(1)  Activities conducted in the normal and ordinary course of agricultural operations or conducted in accordance with sound agricultural practices are presumed to be reasonable and not constitute a nuisance.

(2)  Agricultural operations undertaken in conformity with federal, state, and local laws and regulations, including zoning ordinances, are presumed to be operating within sound agricultural practices.
