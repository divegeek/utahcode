78B-6-310.   Contempt -- Action by court.
The court shall determine whether the person proceeded against is guilty of
the contempt charged.  If the court finds the person is guilty of the
contempt, the court may impose a fine not exceeding $1,000, order the person
incarcerated in the county jail not exceeding 30 days, or both.  However, a
justice court judge or court commissioner may punish for contempt by a fine
not to exceed $500 or by incarceration for five days or both.
