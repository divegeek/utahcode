78B-6-1205.   Summons -- To whom directed.

The summons shall be directed to:

(1)  all joint tenants;

(2)  tenants in common of all persons having any interest in, or recorded liens upon the property or any portion of the property; and

(3)  any other person claiming any interest in the property.
