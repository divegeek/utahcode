78B-16-110.   Duration of abduction prevention order.

An abduction prevention order remains in effect until the earliest of:

(1)  the time stated in the order;

(2)  the emancipation of the child;

(3)  the child's attaining 18 years of age; or

(4)  the time the order is modified, revoked, vacated, or superseded by a court with jurisdiction under Sections 78B-13-201 through 78B-13-203.
