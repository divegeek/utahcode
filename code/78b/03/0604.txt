78B-3-604.   Determination of causation -- Compensation allowed.
(1)  Causation of radiological injury from a nuclear incident shall be
determined by the trier of fact, taking into account epidemiological studies,
statistical probabilities, and other pertinent medical and scientific
evidence.
(2)  A claimant under this part shall be entitled to full compensation of the
claimant's radiological injuries if the trier of fact determines that it is
more likely than not that the claimant's injuries resulted from the nuclear
incident.
