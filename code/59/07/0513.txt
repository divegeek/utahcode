59-7-513.   Interest when time for payment extended.
If the time for payment of the amount determined as the tax by the taxpayer is
extended under the authority of Subsection 59-7-507(2), there shall be
collected as a part of such amount interest at the rate prescribed in Section
59-1-402 from the date when such payment should have been made, if no
extension had been granted, until payment is received.
