59-7-207.   Corporations becoming subject to tax -- Assessment under other
sections.
If a corporation formerly subject to tax under Section 59-7-104 becomes
subject to tax under  this part, it shall file an information return for the
income year in which the change occurs.  The tax for the year in which the
change occurs will be assessed under Section 59-7-104 and not under Section
59-7-201.  For years subsequent to the year in which the change occurs, the
tax will be assessed under Section 59-7-201.
