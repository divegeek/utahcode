59-3-102.   Definitions.
As used in this chapter:
(1)  "Tax equivalent payment" means a payment required or authorized by
statute to be made in lieu of ad valorem taxes on tax exempt property pursuant
to a contract entered into under statutory authority and filed with the county
assessor of the county in which the property is located.
(2)  "Tax equivalent property" means property on which any tax equivalent
payment is made.
