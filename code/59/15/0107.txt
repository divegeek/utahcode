59-15-107.   Report -- Effect of failure to file.
If any person who is liable for the tax and is required by this chapter or by
the rules of the commission to file a report with respect to the beer tax or
to file a report which contains information required to determine the amount
of beer tax, fails, neglects, or refuses to file the report, the commission
shall estimate the amount of beer upon which the tax is payable, and assess
the tax.
