59-12-354.   Collection of tax -- Administrative charge.

(1)  Except as provided in Subsections (2) and (3), the tax authorized under this part shall be administered, collected, and enforced in accordance with:

(a)  the same procedures used to administer, collect, and enforce the tax under:

(i)  Part 1, Tax Collection; or

(ii)  Part 2, Local Sales and Use Tax Act; and

(b)  Chapter 1, General Taxation Policies.

(2) (a) The location of a transaction shall be determined in accordance with Sections 59-12-211 through 59-12-215.

(b) The commission:

(i)  except as provided in Subsection (2)(b)(ii), shall distribute the revenues collected from the tax to the municipality within which the revenues were collected; and

(ii)  shall retain and deposit an administrative charge in accordance with Section 59-1-306 from the revenues the commission collects from a tax under this part.

(3)  A tax under this part is not subject to Section 59-12-107.1 or 59-12-123 or Subsections 59-12-205(2) through (6).
