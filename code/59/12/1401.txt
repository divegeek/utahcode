59-12-1401.   Purpose statement -- Definitions -- Scope of part.
(1)  The purpose of the tax imposed by this part is the same for cities and
towns as is stated in Section 59-12-701 for counties.
(2)  The definitions of Section 59-12-702 are incorporated into this part.
(3)  This part applies only to a city or town that is located within a county
of the second, third, fourth, fifth, or sixth class as designated in Section
17-50-501.
