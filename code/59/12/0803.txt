59-12-803.   Distribution of revenues generated by rural county health care
facilities tax.
(1)  Except as provided in Subsection 59-12-802(5) and Subsection (2), all
revenues collected by a county under Section 59-12-802 shall be distributed
quarterly by the county legislative body to rural county health care
facilities.
(2)  Notwithstanding Subsection (1), if there is more than one rural county
health care facility in a county, the revenues collected by a county under
Section 59-12-802 shall be distributed as determined by the county legislative
body.
