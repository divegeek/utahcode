59-26-103.   Imposition of tax -- Rate.
Subject to Section 59-26-104.5, there is imposed as provided in this part a
tax on the purchaser equal to 6.25% of amounts paid or charged for multi-
channel video or audio service provided by a multi-channel video or audio
service provider:
(1)  within the state; and
(2)  to the extent permitted by federal law.
