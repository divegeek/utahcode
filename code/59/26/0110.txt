59-26-110.   Revenue and Taxation Interim Committee study.
The Revenue and Taxation Interim Committee shall during the 2004 interim:
(1)  study the tax imposed by this chapter;
(2)  recommend whether legislation should be drafted to modify any provision
of this chapter; and
(3)  prepare any legislation that the Revenue and Taxation Interim Committee
recommends in accordance with Subsection (2) for consideration by the
Legislature during the 2005 General Session.
