59-2-901.   Determination of rate by commission -- Transmittal to county and
state auditors.
Before June 22 of each year the commission shall determine the rate of state
tax to be levied and collected upon the taxable value of all property in the
state sufficient to raise the amount of revenue specified by the Legislature
for general state purposes.  That rate may not exceed .00048 per dollar of
taxable value of taxable property in the state.  The commission shall transmit
to the county auditor of each county and to the state auditor a statement of
that rate. The county auditor shall, upon receipt, give the commission written
acknowledgment of receipt.
