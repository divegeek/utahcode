59-2-1334.   Ommission, error, or defect in delinquent lists --
Republication.
(1)  If an omission, error, or defect is in a delinquent list or any
publication, the list or publication may be republished as amended, or notice
of the correction may be given in a supplementary publication.
(2)  Any republication shall be made in the same manner as the original
publication, for not less than one week.
