59-2-1305.   Entries of payments made -- Payments to county treasurer.
(1)  The assessor or, if this duty has been reassigned in an ordinance under
Section 17-16-5.5, the treasurer shall note on the assessment roll, opposite
the names of each person against whom taxes have been assessed, the amount of
the taxes paid.
(2) (a)  The assessor or treasurer, as the case may be, shall require all
checks to be made payable to the office of the county assessor or treasurer,
respectively.
(b)  If the assessor or treasurer receives checks made payable to a payee
other than the office of the county assessor or treasurer, respectively, the
assessor or treasurer, as the case may be, shall immediately endorse the check
with a restrictive endorsement that makes the check payable to the office of
the county treasurer.
(3)  The assessor shall deposit all money the assessor collects into an
account controlled by the county treasurer.
