59-2-1010.   Statement of equalization to be sent to county auditors.

When the equalization among the several counties is completed, the commission shall transmit to each county auditor and to the state auditor a statement of the changes made by it in the assessment books of each county or any assessment contained in the book, which is prima facie evidence of the regularity of all proceedings of the commission resulting in the action which is the subject matter of the statement.
