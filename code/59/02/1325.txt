59-2-1325.   Nature and extent of lien -- Time of attachment -- Effective
date of boundary changes for assessment.
(1) (a)  A tax upon real property is a lien against the property assessed.
(b)  A tax due upon improvements upon real property assessed to a person other
than the owner of the real property is a lien upon the property and
improvements.
(c)  A lien described in Subsection (1)(a) or (b) shall attach on January 1 of
each year.
(2)  An assessment shall be collected in accordance with the effective date
and boundary adjustment provisions in Subsection 17-2-209(4).
