59-2-323.   Changes ordered by commission.
(1)  The commission shall, before June 17 or within ten days after the county
auditors of the state have filed their report with the commission as provided
for under Section 59-2-322, each year transmit to the county auditor a
statement of the changes made by it in the assessment book of the county, as
provided under Section 59-1-210.
(2)  As soon as the county auditor receives from the commission a statement of
the changes made by it in the assessment book of the county, or of any
assessment contained therein, the auditor shall make the corresponding changes
in the assessment book, by entering the same in a column provided with the
proper heading in the assessment book, counting any fractional sum when more
than 50 cents as one dollar and omitting it when less than 50 cents, so that
the value of any separate assessment shall contain no fractions of a dollar;
but shall in all cases disregard any action of the county board of
equalization or commission which is prohibited by law.
