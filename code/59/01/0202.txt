59-1-202.   Qualifications of members of commission.
(1)  Members of the commission shall have:
(a)  significant tax experience that is relevant to holding office as a
commissioner;
(b)  knowledge of tax administration; and
(c)  executive and administrative experience.
(2) (a)  At least one member of the commission shall have expertise in the
theory and practice of ad valorem taxation.
(b)  At least one member of the commission shall have substantial knowledge in
the theory and practice of excise, income, sales, and corporate taxation.
(3)  The membership of the commission shall represent composite skills in
accounting, auditing, property assessment, management, law, and finance.
