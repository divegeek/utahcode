59-1-209.   Official seal -- Authenticated copies of records as evidence.
The commission shall adopt an official seal, and shall file an impression and
description of the seal with the Division of Archives.  Copies of any records
in the possession of the commission may be authenticated with the seal of the
commission attested by the signature of the commission's administrative
secretary, and when so authenticated shall be received in evidence to the same
extent and with the same effect as the originals.
