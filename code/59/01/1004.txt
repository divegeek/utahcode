59-1-1004.   Installment payments.
(1)  The commission may enter into agreements with taxpayers on installment
payments of taxes, penalties, and interest.  The commission may revise,
accelerate, or cancel the installment agreement if any of the following
occurs:
(a)  the commission determines that the financial condition of the taxpayer
has substantially changed;
(b)  the commission determines that the taxpayer provided inaccurate
information concerning his financial condition; or
(c)  the taxpayer fails to make timely payments pursuant to the terms of the
installment agreement.
(2)  The commission shall give the taxpayer reasonable notice of its intent to
revise or cancel an installment agreement entered into under this section.
