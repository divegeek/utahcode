59-1-1305.   Penalty for taxpayer underpayment of tax attributable to a
reportable transaction.
(1)  If the underpayment of a tax by a taxpayer is attributable to a
reportable transaction, the taxpayer is subject to a penalty that is equal to
the product of:
(a)  10%; and
(b)  the amount of the tax underpayment attributable to the reportable
transaction.
(2)  If a taxpayer amends an income tax return to change the tax treatment of
a reportable transaction after the day on which the commission contacts the
taxpayer regarding the examination of the income tax return, that change in
the tax treatment of the reportable transaction contained in the amended
income tax return may not be considered in determining a tax underpayment
under this section.
(3)  A penalty imposed by this section is in addition to any other penalty
imposed by this title.
