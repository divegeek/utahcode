59-1-205.   Chairman -- Quorum -- Voting -- Sessions.
(1)  The governor shall designate one of the members of the commission as
chairperson.
(2) (a)  Three members of the commission constitute a quorum for the
transaction of business.
(b)  A quorum of the commission must participate in any order that constitutes
a final agency action on:
(i)  a formal adjudicative proceeding over which the commission has
jurisdiction;
(ii)  an informal adjudicative proceeding over which the commission has
jurisdiction; or
(iii)  an initial hearing conducted pursuant to Section 59-1-502.5.
(c)  If a commission vote results in a tie vote on any matter described in
Subsection (2)(b), the position of the taxpayer is considered to have
prevailed.
(3)  The commission shall be in session and open for the transaction of
business during ordinary business hours each day.
(4)  The commission may hold sessions or conduct investigations at any place
in the state to facilitate the performance of its duties.
