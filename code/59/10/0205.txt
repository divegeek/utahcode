59-10-205.   Tax on nonresident estate or trust.
(1)  Except as provided in Subsection (2), a tax is imposed on a nonresident
estate or trust in an amount equal to the product of:
(a)  the nonresident estate's or trust's state taxable income as determined
under Section 59-10-204; and
(b)  the percentage listed in Subsection 59-10-104(2).
(2)  The following are not subject to a tax imposed by this part:
(a)  a nonresident estate or trust that is not required to file a federal
income tax return for estates and trusts for the taxable year; or
(b)  a nonresident trust taxed as a corporation.
