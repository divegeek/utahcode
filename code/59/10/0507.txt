59-10-507.   Return by a pass-through entity.
(1)  As used in this section:
(a)  "Pass-through entity" is as defined in Section 59-10-1402.
(b)  "Taxable year" means a year or other time period that would be a taxable
year of a pass-through entity if the pass-through entity were subject to
taxation under this chapter.
(2)  A pass-through entity having any income derived from or connected with
Utah sources shall make a return for the taxable year as prescribed by the
commission.
