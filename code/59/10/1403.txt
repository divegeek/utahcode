59-10-1403.    Income tax treatment of a pass-through entity -- Returns --
Classification same as under Internal Revenue Code.
(1)  Subject to Subsection (3), a pass-through entity is not subject to a tax
imposed by this chapter.
(2)  The income, gain, loss, deduction, or credit of a pass-through entity
shall be passed through to one or more pass-through entity taxpayers as
provided in this part.
(3)  A pass-through entity is subject to the return filing requirements of
Section 59-10-507.
(4)  A pass-through entity that transacts business in the state shall be
classified for purposes of taxation under this title in the same manner as the
pass-through entity is classified for federal income tax purposes.
