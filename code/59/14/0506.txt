59-14-506.   Contraband goods.

Any smokeless tobacco products without affixed warning labels as required by this part, which have been in the possession of any wholesaler, distributor, or retailer in this state for 72 hours or longer, or which have been sold by the wholesaler, distributor, or retailer, are contraband goods.  Those contraband goods may be seized by the commission or its employees, or by any peace officer of the state or its political subdivisions, without a warrant.  The contraband goods shall be destroyed.
