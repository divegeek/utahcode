59-14-213.   Contraband goods.
(1)  Any cigarettes in violation of the requirements of this chapter or of any
state or federal law, including Sections 59-14-203, 59-14-205, 59-14-209,
59-14-211, Subsection 59-14-212(1), or Section 59-14-214 are contraband goods
and may be seized without a warrant by the commission, its employees, or any
peace officer of the state or its political subdivisions.
(2)  Any goods seized pursuant to Subsection (1) shall be delivered to the
commission and destroyed.
(3)  Any cigarettes, as defined in Subsection 59-22-202(4), in violation of
Section 59-14-604 are contraband goods and may be seized in accordance with
Subsections (1) and (2).
