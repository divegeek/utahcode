59-11-112.   Personal representative -- Final account -- Approval by
commission.
No final account of a personal representative in any probate proceeding who is
required to file a federal estate tax return may be allowed and approved by
the court before whom the proceeding is pending unless it is shown by evidence
satisfactory to the commission, and the court finds, that the tax imposed on
the property by this chapter, including applicable interest and penalties, has
been paid in full or that no tax is due.
