17-15-20.   Disposition of coal land revenue.
Eighty per centum of all moneys received by the state of Utah from the
treasurer of the United States, as bonuses, royalties, and rentals upon United
States coal lands located in this state shall be allocated to the county or
counties out of or from which is taken the coal from which such bonuses,
royalties, and rentals are derived; provided, that such sum or sums so
received shall be paid over to such county or counties for the construction
and maintenance of roads and for the support and maintenance of public schools
of such county or counties and no other.
