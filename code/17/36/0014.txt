17-36-14.   Adjustments to tentative budget.
After the public hearing the governing body shall make final adjustments to
the tentative budget as it deems appropriate, giving due consideration to
matters discussed at the hearing. Nevertheless, there shall be no decrease in
the amount appropriated, as provided in Section 17-36-17, for reduction of any
deficit which exists, nor shall any budget increase exceed the estimated
revenue for such budget.
