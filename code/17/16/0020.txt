17-16-20.   Salaries in case of combined offices.
Whenever the county legislative body shall combine the duties of any county
officers the salary of the person discharging the duties of such offices shall
be fixed at a sum not exceeding the highest salary paid to either of the
officers whose offices are so combined, in addition to an amount not exceeding
one-half of the salary fixed for the other officer, when only two offices are
combined, or when more than two offices are combined, in addition to such
highest salary, one-third of the combined salaries of such other officers.
