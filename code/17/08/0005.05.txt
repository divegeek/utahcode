17-8-5.5.   Protection of channels and flood plains -- Acquisition of land.
The county legislative body may also provide by ordinance for the protection
and use of flood channels and present flood plains on rivers, streams, and
canals located within the county and the incorporated municipalities in the
county and may establish by ordinance the boundaries of these flood channels
and present flood plains. The county may acquire and hold by gift or purchase,
such lands, rights of way, easements, or other interests in property within
the established boundaries of these flood channels and present flood plains.
Flood plain as used herein means the lands along the course of the river or
stream which is periodically flooded and for which flood control protective
works would normally be provided or desirable.
