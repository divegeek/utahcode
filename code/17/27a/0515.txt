17-27a-515.   Regulation of residential facilities for persons with disabilities.

A county may only regulate a residential facility for persons with a disability to the extent allowed by:

(1)  Title 57, Chapter 21, Utah Fair Housing Act, and applicable jurisprudence;

(2)  the Fair Housing Amendments Act of 1988, 42 U.S.C. Sec. 3601 et seq., and applicable jurisprudence; and

(3)  Section 504, Rehabilitation Act of 1973, and applicable jurisprudence.
