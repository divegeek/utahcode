17-27a-211.   Canal owner or operator -- Notice to county.
(1)  For purposes of Subsection 17-27a-508(1)(b)(iv), a canal company or a
canal operator shall provide on or before July 1, 2010, any county in which
the canal company or canal operator owns or operates a canal:
(a)  a current mailing address and phone number;
(b)  a contact name; and
(c)  a general description of the location of each canal owned or operated by
the canal owner or canal operator.
(2)  If the information described in Subsection (1) changes after a canal
company or a canal operator has provided the information to the county, the
canal company or canal operator shall provide the correct information within
30 days of the day on which the information was changed.
