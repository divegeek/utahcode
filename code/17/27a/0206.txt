17-27a-206.   Third party notice.
(1)  If a county requires notice to adjacent property owners, the county
shall:
(a)  mail notice to the record owner of each parcel within parameters
specified by county ordinance; or
(b)  post notice on the property with a sign of sufficient size, durability,
print quality, and location that is reasonably calculated to give notice to
passers-by.
(2)  If a county mails notice to third party property owners under Subsection
(1), it shall mail equivalent notice to property owners within an adjacent
jurisdiction.
