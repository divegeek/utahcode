17-19-5.   Numbering of payments -- Payments not presented for collection.
 (1)  Each warrant, check, or other payment mechanism issued during each year,
commencing with the 1st day of January, shall be numbered consecutively, and
the number, date, and the amount of each, the name of the person to whom
payable, and the purpose for which drawn shall be stated thereon. The auditor
shall register all payments when they are made.
(2)  Any payment not presented for collection shall be disposed of pursuant to
Title 67, Chapter 4a, Unclaimed Property Act.
