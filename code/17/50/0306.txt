17-50-306.   Granting franchises over public roads -- Limitation.
(1)  A county may grant franchises along and over the public roads and
highways for all lawful purposes, upon such terms, conditions, and
restrictions as in the judgment of the county legislative body are necessary
and proper, to be exercised in such manner as to present the least possible
obstruction and inconvenience to the traveling public.
(2)  A franchise under Subsection (1) may not be granted for a period longer
than 50 years.
