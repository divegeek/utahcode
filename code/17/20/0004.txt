17-20-4.   Duties of county clerk.
A county clerk shall:
(1)  issue all marriage licenses and keep a register of marriages as provided
by law;
(2)  execute under the clerk's seal and in the name of and for the county, all
deeds and conveyances of all real estate conveyed by the county;
(3)  take and certify acknowledgments and administer oaths;
(4)  keep a fee book as provided by law; and
(5)  take charge of and safely keep the seal of the county, and keep other
records and perform other duties as may be prescribed by law.
