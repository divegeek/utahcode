17-20-5.   Report of election and appointment of officers.

Within 10 days after a county clerk issues a certificate of election or a certificate of appointment made to fill vacancies in elective county offices, the county clerk shall prepare and forward to the lieutenant governor a certified report showing:

(1)  the name of the county;

(2)  the name of the county office to which the person was elected or appointed;

(3)  the date of the election or appointment of the person;

(4)  the date of the expiration of the term for which the person was elected or appointed;

(5)  the date of the certificate of election or appointment; and

(6)  the date of the qualification of the person elected or appointed.



Amended by Chapter 3, 2000 General Session
