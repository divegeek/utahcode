16-6a-1009.   Effect of amendment of articles of incorporation.

(1)  An amendment to the articles of incorporation does not affect:

(a)  any existing right of persons other than members;

(b)  any cause of action existing against or in favor of the nonprofit corporation; or

(c)  any proceeding to which the nonprofit corporation is a party.

(2)  An amendment changing a nonprofit corporation's corporate name does not abate a proceeding brought by or against a nonprofit corporation in its former corporate name.



Enacted by Chapter 300, 2000 General Session
