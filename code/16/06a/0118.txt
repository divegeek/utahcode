16-6a-118.   Electronic documents.
(1)  Notwithstanding the other requirements of this chapter except subject to
Section 16-6a-106, the division may by rule permit a writing required or
permitted to be filed with the division under this chapter:
(a)  to be delivered, mailed, or filed:
(i)  in an electronic medium; or
(ii)  by electronic transmission; or
(b)  to be signed by photographic, electronic, or other means prescribed by
rule, except that a writing signed in an electronic medium shall be signed by
electronic signature in accordance with Title 46, Chapter 4, Uniform
Electronic Transactions Act.
(2)  The division may by rule provide for any writing required or permitted to
be prepared, delivered, or mailed by the division under this chapter to be
prepared, delivered, or mailed:
(a)  in an electronic medium; or
(b)  by electronic transmission.
