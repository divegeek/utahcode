16-10a-701.   Annual meeting.
(1)  A corporation shall hold a meeting of shareholders annually at a time
stated in or fixed in accordance with the bylaws.
(2)  Annual shareholders' meetings may be held in or out of this state at the
place stated in or fixed in accordance with the bylaws.  If no place is stated
in or fixed in accordance with the bylaws, annual meetings shall be held at
the corporation's principal office.
(3)  The failure to hold an annual meeting at the time stated in or fixed in
accordance with a corporation's bylaws does not affect the validity of any
corporate action or work a forfeiture or dissolution of the corporation.
