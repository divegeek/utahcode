16-16-1702.   Relation to Electronic Signatures in Global and National
Commerce Act.
This chapter modifies, limits, or supersedes the federal Electronic Signatures
in Global and National Commerce Act, 15 U.S.C. Sec. 7001 et seq., but does not
modify, limit, or supersede Section 101(c) of that act, 15 U.S.C. Sec.
7001(c), or authorize electronic delivery of any of the notices described in
Section 103(b) of that act, 15 U.S.C. Sec. 7003(b).
