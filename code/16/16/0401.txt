16-16-401.   Authority to amend organic rules.
(1)  A limited cooperative association may amend its organic rules under this
part for any lawful purpose.  In addition, the initial board of directors may
amend the bylaws of an association under Section 16-16-304.
(2)  Unless the organic rules otherwise provide, a member does not have a
vested property right resulting from any provision in the organic rules,
including a provision relating to the management, control, capital structure,
distribution, entitlement, purpose, or duration of the limited cooperative
association.
