16-7-11.   Fees for filing documents and issuing certificates.
The division shall charge and collect a fee determined by it pursuant to
Section 63J-1-504 for:
(1)  filing articles of incorporation of a corporation sole and issuing a
certificate of incorporation;
(2)  filing articles of amendment and issuing a certificate of amendment;
(3)  issuing each additional certificate of incorporation or amendment;
(4)  filing a certificate of authorized agent and issuing the agent's
certificate;
(5)  filing a revocation of authority;
(6)  furnishing a certified copy of any document, instrument, or paper
relating to a corporation sole and affixing its seal;
(7)  issuing a certificate of dissolution; and
(8)  issuing a certificate of merger or consolidation.
