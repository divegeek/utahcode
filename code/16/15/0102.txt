16-15-102.   Definition of business trust.
As used in this chapter:
(1)  "Beneficiary" means a person holding a certificate representing a
beneficial interest in the trust estate and assets.
(2)  "Business trust" has the same meaning as in Section 7-5-1.
(3)  "Division" means the Division of Corporations and Commercial Code.
(4)  "Person" means an individual, general partnership, limited liability
partnership, limited partnership, limited liability company, limited
association, domestic or foreign trust, estate, association, or corporation.
