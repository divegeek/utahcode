34-44-103.   Jurisdiction in courts.
An action under this chapter may be brought against a principal in a court of
this state if:
(1)  the principal enters into a business relationship in this state with a
sales representative to solicit orders for a product or a service; or
(2) (a)  a product of the principal is:
(i)  manufactured, distributed, sold, or received in this state; or
(ii)  imported to or from this state; or
(b)  a service of the principal is provided in this state.
