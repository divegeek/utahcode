34-23-206.   Permitted occupations for minors 10 or older.
Minors 10 years of age or older may work in occupations such as:
(1)  delivery of handbills, newspapers, advertising, and advertising samples;
(2)  shoe-shining;
(3)  gardening and lawn care involving no power-driven lawn or snow removal
equipment;
(4)  caddying; and
(5)  any occupation not determined harmful by the division.
