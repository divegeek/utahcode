34-19-12.   Deputizing of employees prohibited.

No employee of any employer whose employees are on strike or lockout for any reason shall be deputized for any purpose arising from or in connection with such strike by any sheriff, chief of police, town marshal, officer of the highway patrol, or any other peace officer during the time such strike or lockout exists.

Any person who violates the provisions of this section shall be guilty of a misdemeanor.
