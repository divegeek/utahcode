54-8a-7.   Notice of damage -- Repairs.
(1)  If an excavator contacts or damages an underground facility, the
excavator shall:
(a)  immediately notify the appropriate operator and then proceed in a manner
that is reasonably calculated to avoid further damage to the underground
facility; and
(b)  immediately call 911 if the excavation may result in an immediate risk to
human life.
(2)  Upon receipt of notice, the operator shall immediately examine the
underground facility, and, if necessary, make repairs.
