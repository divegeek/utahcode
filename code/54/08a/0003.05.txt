54-8a-3.5.   Excavation-related information included with construction and building permit.

An entity issuing a permit for building or construction that may require excavation may, and is encouraged to, include a notice on or with a permit stating, "Attention, Utah law requires any excavator to notify the owner of underground facilities 48 hours before excavating and comply with Utah Code Title 54, Chapter 8a, Damage to Underground Utility Facilities."



Enacted by Chapter 344, 2008 General Session
