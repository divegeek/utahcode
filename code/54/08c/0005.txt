54-8c-5.   Exemptions.

This chapter does not apply to construction, reconstruction, operation, or maintenance by an authorized person of:

(a)  overhead electrical, cable television, or communications circuits or conductors and their supporting structures;

(b)  electrical generating, transmission, or distribution systems; or

(c)  communications, cable television, or overhead lighting systems.



Enacted by Chapter 250, 1988 General Session
