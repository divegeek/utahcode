54-3-23.   Commission's orders must be obeyed.
Every public utility shall obey and comply with each and every requirement of
every order, decision, direction, rule or regulation made or prescribed by the
commission in the matters herein specified, or in any other matter in any way
relating to or affecting its business as a public utility, and shall do
everything necessary or proper in order to secure compliance with and
observance of every such order, decision, direction, rule or regulation by all
of its officers, agents and employees.
