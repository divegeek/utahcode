54-7-12.1.   Depreciation expense.
In determining the depreciation expense of a telephone corporation in any
proceeding under Section 54-7-12, the commission shall consider all relevant
factors, including the alteration of asset lives to better reflect changes in
the economic life of plant and equipment used to provide telecommunications
services.  A relevant factor to consider shall be the asset lives of existing
and emerging competitive telecommunications providers.  Nevertheless, the
commission shall retain the authority to determine the depreciation expense of
telecommunications corporations for ratemaking purposes.
