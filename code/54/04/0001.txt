54-4-1.   General jurisdiction.
The commission is hereby vested with power and jurisdiction to supervise and
regulate every public utility in this state, and to supervise all of the
business of every such public utility in this state, and to do all things,
whether herein specifically designated or in addition thereto, which are
necessary or convenient in the exercise of such power and jurisdiction;
provided, however, that the Department of Transportation shall have
jurisdiction over those safety functions transferred to it by the Department
of Transportation Act.
