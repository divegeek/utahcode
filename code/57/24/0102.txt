57-24-102.   Restriction on prohibition of display of flag.
(1)  A residential property management authority may not prohibit a resident
from displaying a flag:
(a)  consistent with the guidelines in United States Code Title 4, Chapter 1,
The Flag;
(b)  within an area over which the resident has exclusive control; and
(c)  from a staff, pole, or window.
(2)  In any action to enforce this section, the prevailing party shall be
awarded costs and reasonable attorney fees.
(3)  This section does not apply to a contract or agreement entered into
before May 3, 2004.
