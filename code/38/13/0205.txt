38-13-205.   Priority of liens.
(1)  Except as provided in Subsection (2), a lien imposed under this chapter
has priority over any lien, mortgage, security interest, or other encumbrance
arising after the day on which the lien is effective in accordance with
Section 38-13-201.
(2)  The lien imposed under this chapter does not have priority over a lien
imposed pursuant to Title 59, Chapter 2, Property Tax Act.
