63C-12-105.   Compensation of members -- Expenses.

A member of the council may not receive compensation or benefits for the member's service, but may receive per diem and travel expenses in accordance with:

(1)  Section 63A-3-106;

(2)  Section 63A-3-107; and

(3)  rules made by the Division of Finance pursuant to Sections 63A-3-106 and 63A-3-107.
