63C-13-105.   Authority staff and expenses.

(1)  The Governor's Office of Management and Budget shall:

(a)  provide any necessary staff support for the authority until the authority hires its own staff; and

(b)  cover authority expenses.

(2)  The authority may hire employees, including contract employees and a chief administrative officer.
