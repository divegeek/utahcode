63C-11-315.   Exemptions.
This chapter does not apply to:
(1)  any amateur contest or exhibition of unarmed combat conducted by or
participated in exclusively by:
(a)  a school accredited by the Utah Board of Education;
(b)  a college or university accredited by the United States Department of
Education; or
(c)  any association or organization of a school, college, or university
described in Subsections (1)(a) and (b), when each participant in the contests
or exhibitions is a bona fide student in the school, college, or university;
(2)  any contest or exhibition of unarmed combat conducted in accordance with
the standards and regulations of USA Boxing, Inc.; or
(3)  a white-collar contest.
