7-5-10.   Lending trust funds to trust company, officer, director, or employee as felony.

Unless expressly permitted in the instrument creating a trust account or by a person authorized to give that permission or by a court order as permitted in Section 75-7-802, no trust company shall lend to itself or to any officer or director or employee of the trust company any funds held in any trust account under the powers conferred in this chapter. Any officer, director or employee making such a loan, or to whom such a loan is made, is guilty of a third degree felony.
