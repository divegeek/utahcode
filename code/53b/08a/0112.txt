53B-8a-112.   Tax considerations.
(1)  For tax purposes the property of the Utah Educational Savings Plan Trust
and its income are governed by Section 59-10-201.
(2)  The tax commission, in consultation with the board, may adopt rules
necessary to monitor and implement the tax provisions referred to in
Subsection (1) as related to the property of the Utah Educational Savings Plan
Trust and its income.
