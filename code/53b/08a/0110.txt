53B-8a-110.   Effect of payments on determination of need and eligibility
for student aid.
No student loan program, student grant program, or other program administered
by any agency of the state, except as may be otherwise provided by federal law
or the provisions of any specific grant applicable to that law, shall take
into account and consider amounts available for the payment of higher
education costs pursuant to the plan in determining need and eligibility for
student aid.
