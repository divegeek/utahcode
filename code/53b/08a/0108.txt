53B-8a-108.   Cancellation of agreements.
(1)  Any account owner may cancel an account agreement at will.
(2)  If an account agreement is cancelled by the account owner, the current
account balance shall be disbursed to the account owner less:
(a)  an administrative refund fee, which may be charged by the Utah
Educational Savings Plan Trust, except as provided in Subsection (3); and
(b)  any penalty or tax required to be withheld by the Internal Revenue Code.
(3)  An administration refund fee may not be levied by the Utah Educational
Savings Plan Trust if the account agreement is cancelled due to:
(a)  the death of the beneficiary; or
(b)  the permanent disability or mental incapacity of the beneficiary.
