53B-12-103.   Gifts by persons, corporations, and associations -- Tax
deduction.
(1)  A person, domestic corporation, or association organized for the purpose
of carrying on business in this state may, regardless of the provisions of any
certificate of incorporation, charter, or other articles of organization, make
contributions, gifts, grants, bequests, devises, or loans to the authority.
(2)  The value of the contribution is deductible in computing the net taxable
income of the person, corporation, or association for purposes of an income or
franchise tax imposed by this state or its political subdivisions.
