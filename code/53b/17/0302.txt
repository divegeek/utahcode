53B-17-302.   Receipt for dead body -- Records.

(1)  A person delivering a body to the university under Section 53B-17-301 shall receive a receipt for the body.

(2)  The School of Medicine is responsible for records kept on any body received under this section.

(3)  The records shall include all facts necessary for proper identification of the body.



Enacted by Chapter 167, 1987 General Session
