53B-18-501.   Nonprofit corporations or foundations -- Purpose.
(1)  In addition to any other powers which it now has, Utah State University
may form nonprofit corporations or foundations controlled by the president of
the university and the State Board of Regents to aid and assist the university
in attaining its charitable, scientific, literary, research, and educational
objectives.
(2)  The nonprofit corporations or foundations may receive and administer
legislative appropriations, government grants, contracts, and private gifts to
carry out their public purposes.
