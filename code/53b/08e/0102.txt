53B-8e-102.   Definitions.
As used in this chapter:
(1)  "Purple Heart recipient" means any Utah resident who is a military
veteran and who has earned a Purple Heart award as a result of military
service.
(2)  "State institution of higher education" means an institution listed in
Section 53B-1-102.
(3) (a)  "Tuition" means tuition at the rate for residents of the state.
(b)  "Tuition" excludes fees.
