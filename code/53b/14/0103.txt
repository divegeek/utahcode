53B-14-103.   Failure to receive response or payment after notice --
Authority to collect balance.
If a written response or payment of delinquent installments is not received by
the college, university, or board within 15 days from the date of receipt of
the notice by the borrower, the college, university, or board may determine
the balance due and proceed to collect the balance as provided in Section
53B-14-106.
