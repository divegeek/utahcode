53B-16-201.   Degrees and certificates that may be conferred.

(1)  Utah State University, Snow College, and Salt Lake Community College may confer certificates of completion and degrees as determined by the State Board of Regents.

(2)  The board shall develop evaluative criteria as a means of carefully monitoring the impact of degree programs on the vocational mission of the colleges.



Amended by Chapter 211, 2010 General Session
