65A-4-3.   Mineral leases -- Made exclusively by division -- Disposition of
state mineral lease revenues.
(1)  Mineral leases of all lands owned by the state, except school and
institutional trust lands, shall be made exclusively through the division,
pursuant to division rules, with the consent of the state agency using or
holding the land.
(2) (a)  All revenues from mineral leases of sovereign lands shall be
deposited in the Sovereign Lands Management Account.
(b)  That portion of all revenues from mineral leases on other lands managed
by the division necessary to recover management costs shall be deposited in
the Sovereign Lands Management Account.
(c)  The balance of state mineral lease revenues shall be utilized as directed
by the agency or donor.
