65A-8a-105.   Division to promote implementation of Forest Water Quality
Guidelines.
(1)  The division shall promote implementation of Forest Water Quality
Guidelines before, during, and after the conduct of forest practices on
forested land in order to:
(a)  preserve water quality and soil stability;
(b)  prevent the hazard of fire and insect infestation;
(c)  minimize waste of timber resources; and
(d)  protect the regenerative and productive capacity of forested land.
(2)  The division, in cooperation with Utah State University Extension
Services:
(a)  shall implement a program to develop demonstration areas, books,
brochures, informational material, seminars and workshops, and other aids to
display correct application of the Forest Water Quality Guidelines; and
(b)  may aid private landowners by providing technical assistance to
landowners and operators in implementing Forest Water Quality Guidelines.
