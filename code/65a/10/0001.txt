65A-10-1.   Authority of division to manage sovereign lands.
(1)  The division is the management authority for sovereign lands, and may
exchange, sell, or lease sovereign lands but only in the quantities and for
the purposes as serve the public interest and do not interfere with the public
trust.
(2)  Nothing in this section shall be construed as asserting state ownership
of the beds of nonnavigable lakes, bays, rivers, or streams.
