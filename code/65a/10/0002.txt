65A-10-2.   Recreational use of sovereign lands.
(1)  The division, with the approval of the executive director of the
Department of Natural Resources and the governor, may set aside for public or
recreational use any part of the lands claimed by the state as the beds of
lakes or streams.
(2)  Management of those lands may be delegated to the Division of Parks and
Recreation, the Division of Wildlife Resources, or any other state agency.
