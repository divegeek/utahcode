63A-11-107.   Records access.
(1) (a)  Notwithstanding Title 63G, Chapter 2, Government Records Access and
Management Act, and except as provided in Subsection (1)(b), all records of a
contracted parental defense attorney are protected and may not be released or
made public upon subpoena, search warrant, discovery proceedings, or
otherwise.
(b)  All records of a contracted parental defense attorney are subject to
legislative subpoena, under Title 36, Chapter 14, Legislative Subpoena Powers.
(2)  Records released in accordance with Subsection (1)(b) shall be maintained
as confidential by the Legislature.  The professional legislative staff may,
however, include summary data and nonidentifying information in its audits and
reports to the Legislature.
