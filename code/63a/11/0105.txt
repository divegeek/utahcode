63A-11-105.   Program -- Duties, functions, and responsibilities.

The department shall:

(1)  provide assistance and advice to parental defense attorneys;

(2)  develop and provide educational and training programs for parental defense attorneys; and

(3)  provide information and advice to assist parental defense attorneys to comply with their professional, contractual, and ethical duties.
