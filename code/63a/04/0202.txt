63A-4-202.   Determination of insurance premiums -- Information furnished by
agencies -- Notice to agencies.
(1)  Each agency shall provide the risk manager with all reasonable
information necessary to compute insurance premiums  whenever he requests that
information from them.
(2) (a)  The risk manager shall charge to each agency that receives insurance
coverage from the Risk Management Fund its proportionate share of the cost
incurred based upon actuarially sound rating techniques.
(b)  That premium shall include all costs of operating the fund as stated in
Section 63A-4-201.
(3)  To enable each participating agency to meet its budgeting requirements,
the risk manager shall provide each participating agency with projected
insurance costs for the next two fiscal years within the time limits required.
