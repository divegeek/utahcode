63A-5-501.   Making keys to buildings of state, political subdivisions or
colleges and universities without permission prohibited.
No person shall knowingly make or cause to be made any key or duplicate key
for any building, laboratory, facility, room, dormitory, hall or any other
structure or part thereof owned by the state, by any political subdivision
thereof or by the board of regents or other governing body of any college or
university which is supported wholly or in part by the state without the prior
written consent of the state, political subdivision, board of regents, or
other governing body.
