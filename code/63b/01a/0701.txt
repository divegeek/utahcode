63B-1a-701.   Prior bonds validated -- Exceptions.

All general obligation bonds and bond anticipation notes issued by the commission before February 14, 2003 and all proceedings had in the authorization and issuance of them are:

(1)  validated, ratified, and confirmed; and

(2)  declared to constitute legally binding obligations in accordance with their terms.



Enacted by Chapter 2, 2003 General Session
