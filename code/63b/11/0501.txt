63B-11-501.   State Bonding Commission authorized to issue general
obligation bonds.
Upon receipt of a written opinion from the Utah Attorney General that Salt
Lake County has entered a binding legal agreement with the state in which Salt
Lake County agrees, until all of the principal, interest, and issuance costs
on the bonds have been paid, to annually transfer enough of the 1/4 of 1/4% of
sales tax proceeds earmarked by Section 59-12-502 to the sinking fund created
in Section 63B-11-508 to pay the principal, interest, and issuance costs for
any general obligation bonds issued to provide funds for any of the Salt Lake
County transportation projects identified in Section 63B-11-502, the
commission created under Section 63B-1-201 may issue and sell general
obligation bonds of the state pledging the full faith, credit, and resources
of the state for the payment of the principal of and interest on the bonds, to
provide funds to the Department of Transportation.
