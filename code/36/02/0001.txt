36-2-1.   In-session employees of Legislature enumerated.

The in-session employees of the Legislature shall consist of a secretary of the Senate and a chief clerk of the House, a minute clerk of the Senate and a minute clerk of the House, a docket clerk of the Senate and a docket clerk of the House, a reading clerk of the Senate and a reading clerk of the House, and such other assistants as may be found necessary.



Amended by Chapter 108, 1975 General Session
