36-1-202.   House districts -- Filing -- Legal boundaries.

(1) (a)  The Legislature shall file a copy of the House shapefile enacted by the Legislature with the lieutenant governor's office.

(b)  The legal boundaries of House districts are contained in the House shapefile on file with the lieutenant governor's office.

(2) (a)  The lieutenant governor shall:

(i)  generate maps of each House district from the House shapefile; and

(ii)  ensure that those maps are available for viewing on the lieutenant governor's website.

(b)  If there is any inconsistency between the maps and the House shapefile, the House shapefile is controlling.



Amended by Chapter 382, 2013 General Session
