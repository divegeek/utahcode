77-2-2.   Definitions.

For the purpose of this chapter:

(1)  "Screening" means the process used by a prosecuting attorney to terminate investigative action, proceed with prosecution, move to dismiss a prosecution that has been commenced, or cause a prosecution to be diverted;

(2)  "Diversion" means suspending criminal proceedings prior to conviction on the condition that a defendant agree to participate in a rehabilitation program or make restitution to the victim or fulfill some other condition; and

(3)  "Commencement of prosecution" means the filing of an information or an indictment.
