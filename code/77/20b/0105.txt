77-20b-105.   Revocation of bond.
The surety is entitled to obtain the exoneration of its bond prior to judgment
by providing written proof to the court and the prosecutor that:
(1)  the defendant has been booked for failure to appear regarding the charge
for which the bond was issued; or
(2)  the defendant is in custody and the surety has served the defendant's
bond revocation on the custodial authority.
