77-10a-5.   Grand jurors -- Qualification and selection -- Limits on
disclosure.
(1)  Grand jurors shall meet the qualifications provided for jurors generally
in Title 78B, Chapter 1, Part 1, Jury and Witness Act.  Grand jurors shall be
selected from the qualified jury list as provided in Section 78B-1-107.
(2)  The names of grand jurors are classified as protected records under Title
63G, Chapter 2, Government Records and Access Management Act.
