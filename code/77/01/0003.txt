77-1-3.   Definitions.
For the purpose of this act:
(1)  "Criminal action" means the proceedings by which a person is charged,
accused, and brought to trial for a public offense.
(2)  "Indictment" means an accusation in writing presented by a grand jury to
the district court charging a person with a public offense.
(3)  "Information" means an accusation, in writing, charging a person with a
public offense which is presented, signed, and filed in the office of the
clerk where the prosecution is commenced pursuant to Section 77-2-1.1.
(4)  "Magistrate" means a justice or judge of a court of record or not of
record or a commissioner of such a court appointed in accordance with Section
78A-5-107, except that the authority of a court commissioner to act as a
magistrate shall be limited by rule of the judicial council.  The judicial
council rules shall not exceed constitutional limitations upon the delegation
of judicial authority.
