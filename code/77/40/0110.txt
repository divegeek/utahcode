77-40-110.   Use of expunged records -- Individuals -- Use in civil actions.

Records expunged under this chapter or Section 77-27-5.1 may be released to or viewed by the following individuals:

(1)  the petitioner;

(2)  a law enforcement officer who was involved in the case, for use solely in the officer's defense of a civil action arising out of the officer's involvement with the petitioner in that particular case; and

(3)  parties to a civil action arising out of the expunged incident, providing the information is kept confidential and utilized only in the action.
