77-30-18.   Forfeiture of bail.
If the prisoner is admitted to bail and fails to appear and surrender himself
according to the conditions of his bond the judge or magistrate by proper
order shall declare the bond forfeited and order his immediate arrest without
warrant if he be within this state. Recovery may be had on such bond in the
name of the state as in the case of other bonds given by the accused in
criminal proceedings within this state.
