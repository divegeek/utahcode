77-30-2.   Duty of governor to deliver person charged with crime upon demand
by other state.
Subject to the provisions of this act, the provisions of the Constitution of
the United States controlling, and any and all Acts of Congress enacted in
pursuance thereof, it is the duty of the governor of this state to have
arrested and delivered up to the executive authority of any other state of the
United States any person charged in that state with treason, felony or other
crime who has fled from justice and is found in this state.
