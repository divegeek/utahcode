77-34-7.   Costs and expenses.
(1) Costs arising out of a grant of a furlough for transportation, lodgings,
meals, and other related expenses shall be the sole responsibility of the
furloughee; however, in the event that the furloughee is financially unable to
pay for these expenses, such costs may be assumed by the sending state.
(2)  Extraordinary costs, other than those specified in Subsection (1) arising
from the grant of furlough among party states shall be the sole responsibility
of the sending state. Such costs will generally be confined to emergency
medical and special confinement and transportation needs.
