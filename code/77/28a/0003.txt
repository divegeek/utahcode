77-28a-3.   Duties and powers of courts, departments, agencies and officers in enforcing and effecting compact.

The courts, departments, agencies and officers of this state and its political subdivisions shall enforce this Compact and shall do all things necessary and appropriate to the effectuation of the purposes and intent of this Compact which may be within their respective jurisdictions including, but not limited to, the making and submission of any reports required by that Compact. 



Enacted by Chapter 38, 1982 General Session
