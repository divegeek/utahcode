77-18-6.5.    Liability of rescued person for costs of emergency response.
(1)  Any person who violates Section 76-6-206.1 whose conduct required
emergency care, rescue, assistance, or recovery services at the scene of an
abandoned or inactive mine may be charged with the expenses incurred in
meeting the emergency.
(2)  The court's order shall be a judgment which orders the payment of
reimbursement to any public agency or private body that incurred the expenses.
The judgment shall constitute a lien when recorded in the judgment docket and
shall have the same effect and is subject to the same rules as a judgment for
money in a civil action.
(3)  The liability imposed under this section is in addition to and not in
limitation of any other liability that may be imposed.
