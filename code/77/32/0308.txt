77-32-308.   Pro bono criminal representation -- Liability limits.

Counsel assigned by a court to represent an indigent in criminal, post-conviction, or habeas corpus proceedings is immune from suit if the attorney provides the legal services:

(1)  at no cost; or

(2)  for only a substantially reduced cost that is applied to, but does not cover, expenses of the service; and

(3)  without gross negligence or willful misconduct.
