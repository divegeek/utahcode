63F-1-207.   Coordination within the executive branch -- Cooperation with
other branches.
(1)  In accordance with the executive branch strategic plan and the
requirements of this title, the chief information officer shall coordinate the
development of information technology systems between two or more executive
branch agencies subject to:
(a)  the budget approved by the Legislature; and
(b) Title 63J, Chapter 1, Budgetary Procedures Act.
(2)  In addition to the coordination described in Subsection (1), the chief
information officer shall promote cooperation regarding information technology
in a manner consistent with the interbranch coordination plan created in
accordance with Section 63F-1-201.
