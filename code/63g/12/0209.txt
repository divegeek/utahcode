63G-12-209.   Proficiency standards for English.

(1)  A permit holder shall in good faith use best efforts to become proficient in the English language at or above the equivalent to an intermediate level on a language proficiency assessment test used by the State Office of Education for purposes of secondary school students.

(2)  An undocumented individual shall pay the costs of complying with this section.



Enacted by Chapter 18, 2011 General Session
