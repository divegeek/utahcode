63G-3-202.   Rules having the effect of law.
(1)  An agency's written statement is a rule if it conforms to the definition
of a rule under Section 63G-3-102, but the written statement is not
enforceable unless it is made as a rule in accordance with the requirements of
this chapter.
(2)  An agency's written statement that is made as a rule in accordance with
the requirements of this chapter is enforceable and has the effect of law.
