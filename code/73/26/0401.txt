73-26-401.   Powers of division.

The division may:

(1)  enter into contracts and agreements for the development, operation, maintenance, repair, and replacement of projects authorized under this chapter; and

(2) (a)  set prices for the sale or lease of water made available by the project, in accordance with Section 73-26-506 and rules made by the board; and

(b)  enter into contracts for the sale or lease of the water.
