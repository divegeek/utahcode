73-3b-103.   Prohibitions.
(1)  A person may not artificially recharge an aquifer without first obtaining
a recharge permit.
(2)  A person may not recover from an aquifer water that has been artificially
recharged unless the person first obtains a recovery permit.
(3)  A person holding a recharge permit or recovery permit may not operate a
recharge project or recovery project in a manner that is inconsistent with the
permit conditions set by the state engineer.
