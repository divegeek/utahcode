73-12a-3.   Original compact -- Act as ratified.
The compact and treaty ratified by this act is the original signed by members
of the Colorado River Commission, approved by a representative of the United
States of America, and deposited in the archives of the Department of State of
the United States at Washington, D.C. Any error made in copying the original
compact and treaty as in Section 73-12a-2 hereof shall be held not to
invalidate this ratification or compact and treaty in any way.
