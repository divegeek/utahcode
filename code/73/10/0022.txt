73-10-22.   Water Resources Cities Water Loan Fund -- Annual appropriation -- Interest.

(1)  Beginning with the fiscal year ending June 30, 1978, the Legislature shall provide an annual appropriation from the General Fund from liquor control profits to the Board of Water Resources to make the loans provided for in Sections 73-10-20, 73-10-21, and 73-10-23.  The  money appropriated by the Legislature shall be deposited in a fund known as the Water Resources Cities Water Loan Fund.

(2)  All  money deposited into the Water Resources Cities Water Loan Fund shall be invested by the state treasurer with interest accruing to the Water Resources Cities Water Loan Fund.



Amended by Chapter 183, 1995 General Session
