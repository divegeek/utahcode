73-20-6.   Payment for emergency water resource developments.
The terms and conditions of payment for emergency water resource developments
constructed pursuant to this act shall be set forth in a written contract
prepared by the Division of Water Resources under the direction of the board
and shall be executed by the farmer or rancher benefited. The substantive
terms and conditions for payment shall be specified in the sole discretion of
the board and shall appear in such contract. Title to any emergency water
resource constructed under this act shall remain in the Division of Water
Resources until the actual cost of the project is paid for in full by the
benefited farmer or rancher.
