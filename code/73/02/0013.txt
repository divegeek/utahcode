73-2-13.   Attorney general and county attorneys to counsel.
In all matters requiring legal advice in the performance of his duties and the
prosecution or defense of any action growing out of the performance of his
duties, the attorney general or county attorney of the county in which any
legal question arises, shall be the legal advisers of the state engineer, and
they are hereby required to perform any and all legal services required of
them by him without other compensation than their salaries.
