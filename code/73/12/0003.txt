73-12-3.   Description of land.

The lands subject to this act are located in Utah county, state of Utah, and are described as follows, to wit:

All lands in the bed or on the margin of Utah Lake below elevation 4491.0 (U.S. Coast and Geodetic Survey Adjusted Datum 1922) within the following described boundary:
