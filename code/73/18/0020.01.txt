73-18-20.1.   Seizure of a vessel.
(1)  A peace officer, without a warrant, may seize and take possession of a
vessel:
(a)  that is placed or being operated on the waters of this state with
improper registration;
(b)  that the peace officer has reason to believe has been stolen;
(c)  on which any hull identification number or serial number for an engine or
outboard motor has been defaced, altered, or obliterated;
(d)  that has been abandoned on public land, highways, or waters of this
state; or
(e)  if the registration or title fees for the vessel or outboard motor have
not been paid.
(2)  If necessary for the transportation of a seized vessel, the vessel's
trailer may be seized to transport and store the vessel.
(3)  Any peace officer seizing or taking possession of a vessel under this
section shall comply with the provisions of Section 41-6a-1406.
