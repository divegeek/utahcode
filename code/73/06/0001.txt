73-6-1.   Suspension of right to appropriate -- By proclamation of governor.
For the purpose of preserving the surplus and unappropriated waters of any
stream or other source of water supply for use by irrigation districts and
organized agricultural water users, or for any use whatsoever, when in the
judgment of the governor and the state engineer the welfare of the state
demands it, the governor by proclamation may, upon the recommendation of the
state engineer, suspend the right of the public to appropriate such surplus or
unappropriated waters.
