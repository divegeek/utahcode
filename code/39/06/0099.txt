39-6-99.   Aggravated arson -- Arson.
A person subject to this chapter who willfully and maliciously burns or sets
on fire:
(1)  an inhabitable dwelling, or any other structure, movable or immovable,
and the person knows or has reason to know that at the time there is a human
being within, is guilty of aggravated arson and shall be punished as a
military court directs; and
(2)  the property of another except under Subsection (1), is guilty of arson
and shall be punished as a military court directs.
