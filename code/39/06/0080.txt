39-6-80.   Assault or willful disobedience of subordinate officer.
A warrant officer or enlisted member shall be punished as a military court
directs who:
(1)  strikes or assaults a warrant officer, noncommissioned officer, or petty
officer, while that officer is in the execution of his office;
(2)  willfully disobeys the lawful order of a warrant officer, noncommissioned
officer, or petty officer; or
(3)  treats with contempt or is disrespectful in language or deportment toward
a warrant officer, noncommissioned officer, or petty officer, while that
officer is in the execution of his office.
