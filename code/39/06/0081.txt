39-6-81.   Failure to obey order or regulation.
A person subject to this chapter shall be punished as a military court directs
who:
(1)  violates or fails to obey any lawful general order or regulation;
(2)  knowingly of any other lawful order issued by a member of the National
Guard, which it is his duty to obey, fails to obey the order; or
(3)  is derelict in the performance of his duties.
