39-6-75.   Absence without leave.

A person subject to this chapter shall be punished as a military court directs, who without authority:

(1)  fails to go to his appointed place of duty at the time prescribed;

(2)  leaves his appointed place of duty, or is guilty of desertion;

(3)  absents himself or remains absent from his unit, organization, or place of duty where he is required to be at the time prescribed.



Enacted by Chapter 210, 1988 General Session
