3-1-22.   Accrued rights not affected by chapter.

This act does not impair nor affect any act, offense committed, or right accruing, accrued or acquired, or liability, penalty, forfeiture or punishment incurred prior to the time this act takes effect, but the same may be enjoyed, asserted, enforced, prosecuted, or inflicted as fully and to the same extent as if this act had not been passed.
