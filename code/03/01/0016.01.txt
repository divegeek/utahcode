3-1-16.1.   Resignation of officers.
(1)  An officer may resign by giving written notice of the resignation to the
association.
(2)  A resignation of an officer is effective when the notice is received by
the association, unless the notice specifies a future effective date.
(3)  If a resignation is to be effective as of a future date, the board of
directors may:
(a)  allow the officer to remain in office until that date and fill the
pending vacancy before the effective date, providing that the successor does
not take office until the effective date; or
(b)  remove the officer prior to the effective date and fill the resulting
vacancy.
