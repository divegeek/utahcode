49-20-403.   Assistance to members in purchase of life, health, dental, and medical insurance after retirement -- Employment of personnel to administer section.

(1)  The program may assist active and retired covered individuals and inactive covered individuals of the covered employers to purchase life, health, dental, and medical coverage on a group basis which can be continued after retirement under rules adopted by the board.

(2)  The executive director may employ any personnel, including consultants, to administer this section.



Enacted by Chapter 250, 2002 General Session
