17D-1-306.   Administrative control board review of certain charges in special service districts providing jail service.

If the legislative body of a county of the first class creates an administrative control board under this part for a special service district that provides jail service as provided in Subsection 17D-1-201(10), the administrative control board may review and approve any amount charged to the special service district as reimbursement to the county for services provided under Subsection 17D-1-103(2)(i) before the amount is included in the special service district budget.



Enacted by Chapter 360, 2008 General Session
