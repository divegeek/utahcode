17D-1-211.   Municipality's ability to provide temporary jail facilities not
affected by the creation of a special service district to provide jail
services.
The creation of a special service district to provide jail services as
provided in Subsection 17D-1-201(10) does not affect the ability of a
municipality under Section 10-8-58 to provide, operate, and maintain
facilities for the temporary incarceration, not to exceed 72 hours, of persons
charged with the violation of a municipal ordinance.
