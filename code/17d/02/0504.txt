17D-2-504.   Local building authority bonds payable solely from lease
payments.
(1)  Except to the extent that they are secured as provided in Section
17D-2-505, bonds issued by a local building authority and interest on the
bonds are payable solely from payments received under a lease agreement with
the creating local entity with respect to the project that was financed with
the bond proceeds.
(2)  Each bond issued by a local building authority shall recite on its face
that the bond is payable as provided in Subsection (1).
