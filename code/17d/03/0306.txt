17D-3-306.   Eligibility to vote in an election for board of supervisors
members.
A person is eligible to vote in an election of members of the board of
supervisors of a conservation district if the person:
(1) (a)  is a registered voter; and
(b)  resides within the conservation district; or
(2) (a)  owns or operates private agricultural land in the conservation
district; and
(b) (i)  requests a ballot; or
(ii)  has voted in one of the last two elections of the conservation
district's board of supervisors members.
