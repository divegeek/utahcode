23-20-13.   Signs or equipment -- Damage or destruction unlawful.
 A person may not:
(1)  shoot at, shoot, deface, damage, remove, or destroy any division signs or
placards  located in any part of this state; or
(2)  damage, destroy, remove, or cause to be damaged, destroyed, or removed
any equipment or devices owned, controlled, or operated by the Division of
Wildlife Resources.
