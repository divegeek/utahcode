23-13-7.   Use of fireworks and explosives by division employees and certain federal game agents.

Notwithstanding any other provision of law, employees of the Division of Wildlife Resources and federal game agents charged with the duty of managing wildlife resources may, without obtaining a permit, use fireworks and explosives to rally, drive, or otherwise disperse concentrations of wildlife as may be necessary to protect property or wildlife resources.



Amended by Chapter 76, 1986 General Session
