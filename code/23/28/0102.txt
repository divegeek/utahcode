23-28-102.   Definitions.
As used in this chapter:
(1)  "Migratory bird" is as defined in 16 U.S.C. Sec. 715j.
(2)  "Migratory bird production area" means an area of land that is:
(a)  created under this chapter; and
(b)  used according to the description in Subsections 23-28-201(1)(b)(iii)(A)
through (B).
