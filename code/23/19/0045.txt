23-19-45.   Fees and certificates of registration to harvest brine shrimp
eggs.
(1)  The Wildlife Board may not impose fees to harvest brine shrimp eggs other
than certificate of registration fees.
(2)  Each person holding certificates of registration for the harvesting of
brine shrimp eggs in the 1996-97 harvesting season may obtain the same number
of certificates of registration for the 1997-98 and 1998-99 harvesting seasons
upon payment of the required fee.
