23-19-16.   Licenses obtained from agents of division.
(1)  Licenses provided for in Sections 23-19-17 through 23-19-27 may be
obtained from the division or one of its authorized wildlife license agents.
(2)  Licenses provided for in Sections 23-19-17.5, 23-19-34.7, and 23-19-36
may be obtained only from the division.
