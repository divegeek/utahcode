23-19-42.   Search and rescue surcharge.

(1)  In addition to the fees imposed under this chapter, there is imposed a 25 cent surcharge on each fishing, hunting, or combination license.

(2)  This surcharge shall be deposited in the General Fund as a dedicated credit for the Search and Rescue Financial Assistance Program created under Section 53-2a-1101.



Amended by Chapter 295, 2013 General Session
