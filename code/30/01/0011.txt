30-1-11.   Return of license after ceremony -- Failure -- Penalty.
The person solemnizing the marriage shall within 30 days thereafter return the
license to the clerk of the county whence it issued, with a certificate of the
marriage over his signature, giving the date and place of celebration and the
names of two or more witnesses present at the marriage. For failure to make
such return he shall be guilty of a misdemeanor.
