30-1-39.   Violation of counseling provisions -- Misdemeanor.
Any person coming within the provisions of this act who falsely represents
that he has complied with the requirements of a master plan for premarital
counseling or who, for the purpose of evading the provisions of this act,
applies for a marriage license in a county within the state of Utah which does
not require premarital counseling, is guilty of a misdemeanor.
