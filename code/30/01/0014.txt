30-1-14.   Acting without authority -- Impersonation -- Forgery -- Penalty.
A person is guilty of a third degree felony if he:
(1)  knowingly solemnizes a marriage in violation of either Section 30-1-6,
30-1-7, or 30-1-9.1;
(2)  impersonates a parent or guardian of a minor to obtain a license for the
minor to marry; or
(3)  forges the name of a parent or guardian of a minor on any writing
purporting to give consent to a marriage of a minor.
