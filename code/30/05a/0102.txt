30-5a-102.   Definitions.
As used in this chapter:
(1)  "Parent" means a biological or adoptive parent.
(2)  "Person other than a parent" means a person related to the child by
marriage or blood, including:
(a)  siblings;
(b)  aunts;
(c)  uncles;
(d)  grandparents; or
(e)  current or former step-parents, or any of the persons in Subsections
(2)(a) through (d) in a step relationship to the child.
