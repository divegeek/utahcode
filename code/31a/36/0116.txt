31A-36-116.   Other law enforcement or regulatory authority.
This chapter does not:
(1)  preempt the authority or relieve the duty of other law enforcement or
regulatory agencies to investigate, examine, and prosecute suspected
violations of law;
(2)  prevent or prohibit a person from disclosing voluntarily information
concerning life settlement fraud to a law enforcement or regulatory agency
other than the insurance department; or
(3)  limit the powers granted elsewhere by law to the commissioner or an
insurance fraud unit to investigate and examine possible violations of law and
to take appropriate action.
