31A-5-401.1.   Definitions.
As used in this Part 4:
(1)  "Voting members of mutuals" and "voting members" mean persons entitled to
vote at annual or special meetings of the members of a mutual, as set forth in
the articles of incorporation or amendments to the articles.
(2)  Voting members of mutuals and voting members do not necessarily include
policyholders of the mutual, if the articles of incorporation or amendments to
the articles of incorporation so provide, and if the articles of incorporation
or amendments have been approved by the commissioner after a hearing.
