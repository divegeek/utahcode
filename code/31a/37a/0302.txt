31A-37a-302.   Minimum capital and surplus.
(1)  The commissioner may not issue a special purpose financial captive
insurance company a certificate of authority under this chapter unless it
possesses unimpaired paid-in capital and surplus of not less than $250,000 on
the day on which the certificate of authority is issued.
(2)  A special purpose financial captive insurance company shall maintain
unimpaired paid-in capital and surplus of not less than $250,000 at all times
when having a certificate of authority under this chapter.
