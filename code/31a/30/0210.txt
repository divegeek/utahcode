31A-30-210.   State contract requirements -- Employer default plans.
(1)  This section applies to an employer who is required to offer its
employees a health benefit plan as a condition of qualifying for a state
contract under:
(a)  Section 17B-2a-818.5;
(b)  Section 19-1-206;
(c)  Subsection 63A-5-205(3);
(d)  Section 63C-9-403;
(e)  Section 72-6-107.5; and
(f)  Section 79-2-404.
(2)  An employer described in Subsection (1) shall, when selecting the default
plan required in Section 31A-30-204, select a default plan that is "qualified
health insurance coverage" as defined in the sections listed in Subsections
(1)(a) through (f).
