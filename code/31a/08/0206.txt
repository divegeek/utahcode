31A-8-206.   Powers under organization permit -- Deposit of proceeds of subscriptions.

Sections 31A-5-207 and 31A-5-208 apply to the powers of an organization under an organization permit and the deposit of proceeds of subscriptions, except that there are no qualifying insurance policies as referred to in Subsection 31A-5-207(2)(a).



Enacted by Chapter 204, 1986 General Session
