31A-14-213.   Transfer of business.
(1)  A foreign insurer that intends to transfer to another person all or a
substantial part of its insurance business in Utah by means of an assumption
reinsurance transaction, a sale, or otherwise, shall report the proposed
transaction to the commissioner not less than 30 days before the proposed
effective date.  The commissioner may approve the transaction to take effect
immediately.  Alternatively, the commissioner may defer the effective date of
the transaction for an additional period not exceeding 30 days, by written
notice to the insurer before the expiration of the initial 30-day period.
(2)  The commissioner may, within the 30-day period or its extension, prohibit
the proposed action if it would be contrary to the law or to the interests of
insureds, creditors, or the public in Utah.
