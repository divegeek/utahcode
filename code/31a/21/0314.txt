31A-21-314.   Prohibited provisions.
No insurance policy subject to this chapter may contain any provision:
(1)  requiring it to be construed according to the laws of another
jurisdiction except as necessary to meet the requirements of compulsory
insurance laws of other jurisdictions;
(2)  depriving Utah courts of jurisdiction over an action against the insurer,
except as provided in permissible arbitration provisions; or
(3)  limiting the right of action against the insurer to less than three years
from the date the cause of action accrues.
