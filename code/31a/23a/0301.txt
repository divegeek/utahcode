31A-23a-301.   Agency license.

An insurance organization shall be licensed as an agency if the insurance organization acts as:

(1)  a producer;

(2)  a surplus lines producer;

(3)  a limited line producer;

(4)  a consultant;

(5)  a managing general agent; or

(6)  a reinsurance intermediary.
