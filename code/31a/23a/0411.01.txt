31A-23a-411.1.    Person's liability if premium received is not forwarded to
the insurer.
A person commits insurance fraud as described in Subsection 31A-31-103(1)(f)
if that person knowingly fails to forward to the insurer a premium:
(1)  received from one of the following in partial or total payment of the
premium due from:
(a)  an applicant;
(b)  a policyholder; or
(c)  a certificate holder; or
(2)  collected from or on behalf of an insured employee under an insured
employee benefit plan.
