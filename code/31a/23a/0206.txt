31A-23a-206.   Special requirements for variable contracts line of authority.

(1)  Before applying for a variable contracts line of authority:

(a)  a producer shall be licensed under Section 61-1-3 as a:

(i)  broker-dealer; or

(ii)  broker-dealer agent; and

(b)  a consultant shall be licensed under Section 61-1-3 as an:

(i)  investment adviser; or

(ii)  investment adviser representative.

(2) A producer's or consultant's variable contracts line of authority is revoked on the day the producer's or consultant's securities related license under Section 61-1-3 is no longer valid.
