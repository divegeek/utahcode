31A-23a-205.   Special requirements for bail bond producers and bail bond enforcement agents.

(1)  As used in this section, "bail bond producer" and "bail enforcement agent" have the same definitions as in Section 31A-35-102.

(2)  A bail bond producer may not operate in this state without an appointment from one or more authorized bail bond surety insurers or licensed bail bond surety companies.

(3)  A bail bond enforcement agent may not operate in this state without an appointment from one or more licensed bail bond producers.
