31A-20-109.   Single risk limitation for title insurance.
(1)  As used in this section:
(a)  "Net retained liability" means the total potential liability retained by
a title insurer for a single risk, after deducting liability reinsured for
which credit may be taken under Section 31A-17-404.
(b)  "Single risk" means the sum of the potential liabilities under all title
insurance policies issued on any estates in the same real property.
(2)  The net retained liability of a title insurer for a single risk, whether
assumed directly or as reinsurance, may not exceed 50% of the capital and
surplus of the insurer.
