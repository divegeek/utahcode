31A-22-519.   Death pending conversion.
If a person insured under a group life insurance policy, or the insured
dependent of that person, dies during the period of eligibility for conversion
under Section 31A-22-517 or 31A-22-518 and before the individual policy
becomes effective, the amount of life insurance to which he would have been
entitled is payable as a claim under the group policy, whether or not
application for the individual policy or the payment of the first premium has
been made.
