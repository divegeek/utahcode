31A-22-301.   Definitions.

As used in this part:

(1)  "Motor vehicle" has the same meaning as under Subsection 41-12a-103(4).

(2)  "Motor vehicle business" means a motor vehicle sales agency, repair shop, service station, storage garage, or public parking place.

(3)  "Motor vehicle liability policy" means a policy which satisfies the requirements of Sections 31A-22-303 and 31A-22-304.

(4)  "Occupying" means being in or on a motor vehicle as a passenger or operator, or being engaged in the immediate acts of entering, boarding, or alighting from a motor vehicle.

(5)  "Operator" has the same meaning as under Subsection 41-12a-103(7).

(6)  "Owner" has the same meaning as under Subsection 41-12a-103(8).

(7)  "Pedestrian" means any natural person not occupying a motor vehicle.
