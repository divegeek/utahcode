31A-12-107.   Governmental immunity.
Notwithstanding any other provision of this title, a governmental entity is
not an insurer for purposes of this title and is not engaged in the business
of insurance to the extent that it is:
(1)  covering its own liabilities under Title 63G, Chapter 7, Governmental
Immunity Act of Utah; or
(2)  engaging in other related risk management activities related to the
normal course of its activities.
