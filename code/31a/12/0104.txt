31A-12-104.   Insurance policies issued to school districts.

Insurance policies issued by the Risk Management Fund to school districts under Section 63A-4-204 shall conform to Chapters 21 and 22. Policy forms issued to the school district shall be filed under Section 31A-21-201. The policy form filing fees of Section 31A-3-103 shall be paid to the Insurance Department by the Risk Management Fund.
