31A-2-201.1.   General filing requirements.

Except as otherwise provided in this title, the commissioner may set by rule made in accordance with Title 63G, Chapter 3, Utah Administrative Rulemaking Act, specific requirements for filing any of the following required by this title:

(1)  a form;

(2)  a rate; or

(3)  a report.
