31A-25-207.   Form and contents of license.
(1)  Licenses issued under this chapter shall be in the form the commissioner
prescribes and shall set forth:
(a)  the name, address, and telephone number of the licensee;
(b)  the date of license issuance; and
(c)  any other information the commissioner considers advisable.
(2)  A third party administrator doing business under any other name than the
administrator's legal name shall notify the commissioner prior to using the
assumed name in this state.
(3) (a)  An organization shall be licensed as an agency if the organization
acts as a third party administrator.
(b)  An agency license issued under Subsection (3)(a) shall set forth the
names of all natural persons licensed under this chapter who are authorized to
act in those capacities for the organization in this state.
