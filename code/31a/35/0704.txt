31A-35-704.   Submission of bail bond sureties and producers to jurisdiction of court.

By applying for and receiving a license or certificate to engage in the bail bond surety insurance business in accordance with this chapter, a bail bond surety or bail bond producer:

(1) submits to the jurisdiction of the court;

(2)  irrevocably appoints the clerk of the court as agent upon whom any papers affecting the bail bond surety's or bail bond producer's liability on the undertaking may be served; and

(3) acknowledges that liability may be enforced on motion and upon notice as the court may require, without the necessity of an independent action.
