31A-9-103.   Orders imposing and relaxing restrictions.
(1)  The commissioner may subject any fraternal to some or all of the
restrictions of Subsections 31A-5-305 (2)(a)(i) and (ii), and Subsection
31A-5-410 (1)(b), as such provisions are incorporated by Sections 31A-9-303
and 31A-9-407.
(2)  The commissioner may free a fraternal from any of the restrictions
applicable to fraternals under the provisions enumerated in Subsection (1), if
he is satisfied that the fraternal's financial condition, management, and
other circumstances give assurance that the interests of insureds and the
public will not be endangered by the waiver.
