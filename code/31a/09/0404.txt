31A-9-404.   Annual report to fraternal members.

(1)  Every domestic fraternal shall send to each member having insurance or shall publish in the official publication under Subsection 31A-9-402(1), an abbreviated annual report.

(2)  This report shall contain:

(a)  basic financial and operating data;

(b)  information about important business and corporate developments;

(c)  other information the fraternal wishes to include; or

(d)  other information the commissioner by rule requires it to include to adequately inform its members.

(3)  The fraternal shall send a business mail reply form on which the member may request a more complete annual report. This complete annual report shall contain the information prescribed by rule under Subsection 31A-2-201(3).
