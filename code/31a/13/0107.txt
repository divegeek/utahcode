31A-13-107.   Commissioner's remedies.

If the trustees of any employee welfare fund have failed to register the fund in accordance with Section 31A-13-103, or otherwise fail to comply with this chapter, the commissioner shall notify the employers of the failure.  In addition to ordering compliance under Subsection 31A-2-201(4), the commissioner may:

(1)  order the employers to stop making payments to the trustees until the employers are notified by the commissioner that the trustees are in compliance with this chapter; or

(2)  rehabilitate or liquidate the employee welfare fund under Chapter 27a, Insurer Receivership Act.
