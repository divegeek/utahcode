31A-13-101.   Scope.
This chapter applies to every employee welfare fund covering any person
employed in this state except to the extent that:
(1)  federal law excludes the operation of state law;
(2)  the fund qualifies for exemption from regulation under a rule adopted by
the commissioner defining funds which provide no benefits involving insurance
risks; or
(3)  the commissioner determines by rule that the insurance-like risks are
minimal or present no problems requiring active regulation.
