34A-2-404.   Injuries to minors.
(1)  A minor is considered sui juris for the purposes of this chapter and
Chapter 3, Utah Occupational Disease Act, and no other person shall have any
cause of action or right to compensation for an injury to the minor employee.
(2)  Notwithstanding Subsection (1), in the event of the award of a lump sum
of compensation to a minor employee, the sum shall be paid only to the minor's
legally appointed guardian.
