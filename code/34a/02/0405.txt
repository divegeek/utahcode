34A-2-405.   Employee injured outside state -- Entitled to compensation --
Limitation of time.
(1)  Except as provided in Subsection (2), if an employee who has been hired
or is regularly employed in this state receives personal injury by accident
arising out of and in the course of employment outside of this state, the
employee, or the employee's dependents in case of the employee's death, shall
be entitled to compensation according to the law of this state.
(2)  This section applies only to those injuries received by the employee
within six months after leaving this state, unless prior to the expiration of
the six-month period the employer has filed with the division notice that the
employer has elected to extend such coverage a greater period of time.
