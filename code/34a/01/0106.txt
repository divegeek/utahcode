34A-1-106.   Fees.

(1)  Unless otherwise provided by statute, the commission may adopt a schedule of fees assessed for services provided by the commission by following the procedures and requirements of Section 63J-1-504.

(2)  The commission shall submit each fee established under this section to the Legislature for its approval as part of the commission's annual appropriations request.
