72-7-204.   Issuance of licenses -- Fees -- Duration -- Renewal --
Disposition of proceeds.
(1)  The department has the sole authority to issue licenses for the
establishment, maintenance, and operation of junkyards within the limits
defined in Section 72-7-203, and shall charge a $10 license fee payable
annually in advance.
(2)  All licenses issued under this section expire on the first day of January
following the date of issue.  Licenses may be renewed from year to year upon
payment of the requisite fee.
(3)  Proceeds from the license fee shall be deposited with the state treasurer
and credited to the Transportation Fund.
