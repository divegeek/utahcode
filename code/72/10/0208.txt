72-10-208.   Providing for levying of taxes.
The local public authorities having power to appropriate money within the
counties, municipalities, or other public subdivisions of this state for the
purpose of acquiring, establishing, developing, operating, maintaining, or
controlling airports  under the provisions of this part, are authorized to
appropriate and cause to be raised by taxation or otherwise in such political
subdivisions money sufficient to carry out therein the provisions of this
part, also to use for such purpose or purposes money derived from the
airports.
