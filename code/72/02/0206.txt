72-2-206.   Department authority to contract.

The department may, upon approval of the commission:

(1)  make all contracts, execute all instruments, and do all things necessary or convenient to provide financial assistance for transportation projects in accordance with this chapter; and

(2)  enter into and perform the contracts and agreements with entities concerning the planning, construction, lease, or other acquisition, installation, or financing of transportation projects.
