72-4-206.   The Utah Fruitway.

(1)  There is established the Utah Fruitway comprising a section of Route 89 beginning at the Box Elder and Weber County line north to the south city limit of Brigham City.

(2)  In addition to other official designations, the Department of Transportation shall designate the portions of the highway identified in Subsection (1) as the Utah Fruitway on future state highway maps.



Enacted by Chapter 66, 2002 General Session
