72-4-208.   James V. Hansen Highway.

(1)  There is established the James V. Hansen Highway comprising a section of Route 89 beginning in Farmington at the Route 89 and Interstate Highway 15 interchange north to Route 203, Harrison Boulevard in South Ogden.

(2)  In addition to other official designations, the Department of Transportation shall designate the portions of the highway identified in Subsection (1) as the James V. Hansen Highway on future state highway maps.



Enacted by Chapter 130, 2003 General Session
