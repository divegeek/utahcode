17C-3-401.   Agency to provide notice of hearings.

(1)  Each agency shall provide notice, as provided in this part, of each:

(a)  plan hearing; and

(b)  budget hearing.

(2)  The notice required under Subsection (1) for a plan hearing may be combined with the notice required for a budget hearing if those two hearings are combined under Section 17C-3-301.
