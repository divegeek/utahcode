70D-2-305.   Fee restrictions.
A lender or broker may not accept a fee or deposit from an applicant for a
mortgage loan unless at the time the lender or broker accepts the fee or
deposit there is a written statement:
(1)  signed by the applicant;
(2)  stating whether or not the fee or deposit is refundable; and
(3)  describing the conditions, if any, under which all or a portion of the
fee or deposit will be refunded to the applicant.
