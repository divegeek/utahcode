32B-12-203.   Renewal requirements for liquor warehousing license.

(1)  A liquor warehousing license expires on December 31 of each year.

(2)  To renew a liquor warehousing license, a person shall submit to the department by no later than November 30 of the year the license expires:

(a)  a completed renewal application in a form prescribed by the department; and

(b)  a renewal fee of $1,200.

(3)  Failure to meet the renewal requirements results in an automatic forfeiture of the liquor warehousing license effective on the date the existing liquor warehousing license expires.
