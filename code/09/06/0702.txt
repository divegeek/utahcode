9-6-702.   Definitions.
As used in this part:
(1)  "Committee" means the State-Owned Art Collections Inventory Committee
established under Section 9-6-704.
(2)  "Program" means the State-Owned Art Collections Inventory Study Program
established under Section 9-6-703.
(3) (a)  "State" means the state of Utah, its executive, legislative, and
judicial branches, its departments, divisions, agencies, boards, commissions,
councils, committees, and institutions including state institutions of higher
education as defined under Section 53B-3-102.
(b)  "State" does not include:
(i)  the political subdivisions of the state; or
(ii)  school districts.
(4)  "Work of art" means visual art of any medium.
