9-6-606.   Office limitations.

The office may not:

(1)  collect, manage, borrow, or purchase artifacts, objects, or other items for its own collection;

(2)  operate its own exhibit or display area; or

(3)  direct any action to be taken or make any mandatory policies to be followed by an individual museum or its parent agency.
