9-6-504.   Duties of board.
The board shall:
(1)  allocate moneys from the state fund to the endowment fund created by a
qualifying organization under Section 9-6-503;
(2)  determine the eligibility of each qualifying organization to receive
moneys from the state fund into the endowment fund of the qualifying
organization and be the final arbiter of eligibility;
(3)  determine the matching amount each qualifying organization must raise in
order to qualify to receive moneys from the state fund;
(4)  establish a date by which each qualifying organization must provide its
matching funds;
(5)  verify that matching funds have been provided by each qualifying
organization by the date determined in Subsection (4); and
(6)  in accordance with Title 63G, Chapter 3, Utah Administrative Rulemaking
Act, establish criteria by rule not otherwise prescribed in this chapter for
determining the eligibility of qualifying organizations to receive moneys from
the state fund.
