9-8-307.   Report of discovery on state or private lands.
(1)  Any person who discovers any archaeological resources on lands owned or
controlled by the state or its subdivisions shall promptly report the
discovery to the division.
(2)  Any person who discovers any archaeological resources on privately owned
lands shall promptly report the discovery to the division.
(3)  Field investigations shall be discouraged except in accordance with this
part and Part 4.
(4)  Nothing in this section may be construed to authorize any person to
survey or excavate for archaeological resources.
