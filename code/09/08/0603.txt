9-8-603.   Standards -- Publication.

(1)  The writing and publishing of each history shall meet good historiographical and other professional standards as prescribed and approved by the division.  The history shall be identified as the Centennial History of the County.

(2)  After approval by the division, each county history may bear, with the approval of the Utah State Centennial Commission, the state centennial history logo and shall be compiled, published, and offered to the public before December 31, 1996, as part of the county's and state's centennial celebrations at a cost not to exceed the cost of printing and binding.  The proceeds of the sales shall be returned to the county.



Enacted by Chapter 30, 1991 General Session
