9-7-303.   Withdrawing books.
(1)  Books may be taken from the State Law Library by:
(a)  the members, officers, and staff of the Legislature;
(b)  the officers and staff of the executive departments and of the several
boards and commissions of the state government; and
(c)  the justices of the Supreme Court, the judges of other state courts, and
their staffs.
(2)  No other person may withdraw any book from the State Law Library.
