9-7-201.   State Library Division -- Creation -- Purpose.
(1)  There is created within the department the State Library Division under
the administration and general supervision of the executive director or the
designee of the executive director.
(2)  The division shall be under the policy direction of the board.
(3)  The division shall function as the library authority for the state and is
responsible for general library services, extension services, the
preservation, distribution and exchange of state publications, legislative
reference, and other services considered proper for a state library.
