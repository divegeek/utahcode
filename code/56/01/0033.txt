56-1-33.   First-aid kits -- Failure to comply with order or regulation -- Penalty.

Any person failing to comply with an order or regulation of the commission authorized by this act shall be liable to a penalty of $25 for each day of noncompliance. The attorney general shall file suit on behalf of the state for any unpaid penalty within one year after the penalty accrues.



Enacted by Chapter 131, 1961 General Session
