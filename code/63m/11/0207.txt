63M-11-207.   Oversight -- Executive director salary -- Staff support -- Use
of funds.
(1)  The Center on Aging shall:
(a)  pay the salary, and oversee the performance of, the executive director of
the commission;
(b)  provide staff support for the executive director of the commission and
the commission; and
(c)  provide office space, furnishings, and supplies to the commission, the
executive director of the commission, and support staff.
(2)  The funds appropriated by the Legislature for the commission may only be
used for the purposes described in this chapter.
