63M-1-1106.   Eligibility review.
(1)  The office shall:
(a)  review and evaluate an application submitted under Section 63M-1-1104;
and
(b)  determine whether the municipality or county is eligible for designation
as a recycling market development zone.
(2)  In designating recycling market development zones, the office shall
consider:
(a)  whether the current waste management practices and conditions of the
county or municipality are favorable to the development of postconsumer waste
material markets;
(b)  whether the creation of the zone is necessary to assist in attracting
private sector recycling investments to the area; and
(c)  the amount of available landfill capacity to serve the zone.
