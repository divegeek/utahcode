63M-1-907.   Agreements.
The administrator shall enter into agreements with each successful applicant
that have specific terms and conditions for each loan or assistance,
including:
(1)  repayment schedules;
(2)  interest rates;
(3)  specific economic activity required to qualify for the loan or assistance
or for repayment credits;
(4)  collateral or security, if any; and
(5)  other terms and conditions considered appropriate by the administrator.
