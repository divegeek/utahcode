63M-4-102.   Definitions.

As used in this chapter:

(1)  "Energy advisor" means the governor's energy advisor appointed under Section 63M-4-401.

(2)  "Office" means the Office of Energy Development created in Section 63M-4-401.

(3)  "State agency" means an executive branch:

(a)  department;

(b)  agency;

(c)  board;

(d)  commission;

(e)  division; or

(f)  state educational institution.
