13-25a-105.   Penalties -- Administrative and criminal.
(1)  Any person who violates this chapter is subject to:
(a)  a cease and desist order; and
(b)  an administrative fine of not less than $100 or more than $2,500 for each
separate violation.
(2)  All administrative fines collected under this chapter shall be deposited
in the Consumer Protection Education and Training Fund created in Section
13-2-8.
(3)  Any person who intentionally violates this chapter is guilty of a class
A misdemeanor and may be fined up to $2,500.  A person intentionally violates
this chapter if the violation occurs after the division, attorney general, or
a district or county attorney notifies the person by certified mail that he is
in violation of this chapter.
