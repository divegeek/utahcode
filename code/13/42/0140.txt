13-42-140.   Transitional provisions -- Application to existing
transactions.
(1)  Transactions entered into before July 1, 2007 and the rights, duties, and
interests resulting from them may be completed, terminated, or enforced as
required or permitted by a law amended, repealed, or modified by this chapter
as though the amendment, repeal, or modification had not occurred.
(2) (a)  A person registered under Chapter 21, Credit Services Organizations
Act, on June 30, 2007, that is required to be registered under this chapter on
July 1, 2007, shall be considered to be registered under this chapter until
the license in effect on June 30, 2007, expires.
(b)  Notwithstanding Subsection (2)(a), except for the registration
requirement, a person subject to this chapter shall comply with this chapter
for any transaction entered into on or after July 1, 2007.
