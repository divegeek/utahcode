13-1-6.   Rules and regulations.
(1)  The executive director shall prescribe rules and procedures for the
management and operation of the department, the conduct of its employees, and
the custody, use, and preservation of its records, papers, books, documents,
and property.
(2)  The department and its divisions, in contemplation, formulation, and
passage of rules pursuant to Subsection (1), shall acknowledge and consider
the facilitation of commerce in all its forms, including reliable electronic
commerce, for the benefit of both consumers and businesses.
