13-40-401.   Phishing and pharming violations.
(1)  A civil action against a person who violates any provision of Part 2,
Phishing and Pharming, may be filed by:
(a)  an Internet service provider that is adversely affected by the violation;
(b)  an owner of a webpage, computer server, or a trademark that is used
without authorization in the violation; or
(c)  the attorney general.
(2)  A person permitted to bring a civil action under Subsection (1) may
obtain either actual damages for a violation of this chapter or a civil
penalty not to exceed $150,000 per violation of Part 2, Phishing and Pharming.
(3)  A violation of Part 2, Phishing and Pharming, by a state-chartered or
licensed financial institution is enforceable exclusively by the financial
institution's primary state regulator.
