13-34-201.   Fraudulent educational credentials.
(1)  A person may not use, give, or receive, or attempt or conspire to do so,
in connection with a business, trade, profession, or occupation, a degree or
other document which has been purchased, obtained, fraudulently or illegally
issued, counterfeited, materially altered, or found, or which serves to
evidence the undertaking or completion of scholastic achievement if the
education has not been undertaken and attained.
(2)  A violation of this section is a class A misdemeanor.
