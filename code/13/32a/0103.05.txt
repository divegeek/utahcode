13-32a-103.5.   Applicability to coin dealers -- Specie legal tender exempt from chapter.

(1)  This chapter applies to coin dealers, except:

(a)  where provisions otherwise specifically address coin dealers; or

(b)  as provided in Subsection (2).

(2)  Specie legal tender as defined in Section 59-1-1501.1 that is used as legal tender is exempt from this chapter.
