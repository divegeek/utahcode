46-1-4.   Bond.
(1)  A notarial commission may not become effective until a constitutional
oath of office and a $5,000 bond has been filed with and approved by the
lieutenant governor.  The bond shall be executed by a licensed surety for a
term of four years commencing on the commission's effective date and
terminating on its expiration date, with payment of bond funds to any person
conditioned upon the notary's misconduct while acting in the scope of his
commission.
(2)  The bond required under Subsection (1) may be executed by the Office of
Risk Management for notaries public employed by a state office or agency.
