46-1-21.   Resignation.
(1)  A notary who resigns a notarial commission shall provide to the
lieutenant governor a notice indicating the effective date of resignation.
(2)  A notary who ceases to reside in this state or who becomes unable to read
and write as provided in Section 46-1-3 shall resign the commission.
(3)  A notary who resigns shall destroy the official seal and certificate.
