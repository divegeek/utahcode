79-3-509.   Curriculum and materials for the training of volunteers who
assist paleontologists.
(1)  The survey shall develop a curriculum and materials for the training of
volunteers who assist paleontologists in the field and laboratory.
(2)  The director shall appoint a qualified survey employee to develop the
curriculum and materials under this section.
(3)  The survey may request input and assistance from any interested
organization in developing the curriculum and materials.
(4)  The survey may collect fees to cover the costs of the materials and
updating of the curriculum.
