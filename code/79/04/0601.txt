79-4-601.   "This is the Place Monument," Camp Floyd, old statehouse -- Supervision and control.

(1)  The division shall administer and supervise the following historical monuments: 

(a)  "This is the Place Monument" site;

(b)  Camp Floyd; and

(c)  the old statehouse at Fillmore.

(2)  The division may make expenditures for maintenance and administration from funds:

(a)  made available by appropriation; or

(b)  that are available to the division.
