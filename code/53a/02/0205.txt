53A-2-205.   Permitting attendance by nonresident of the state -- Tuition.
(1)  A local school board may permit a child residing outside the state to
attend school within the district.  With the exception of a child enrolled
under Section 53A-2-206, the child is not included for the purpose of
apportionment of state funds.
(2)  The board shall charge the nonresident child tuition at least equal to
the per capita cost of the school program in which the child enrolls unless
the board, in open meeting, determines to waive the charge for that child in
whole or in part.  The official minutes of the meeting shall reflect the
determination.
