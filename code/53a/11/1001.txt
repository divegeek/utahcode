53A-11-1001.   Notification by juvenile court and law enforcement agencies.
(1)  Notifications received from the juvenile court or law enforcement
agencies by the school district pursuant to Subsections 78A-6-112(3)(b) and
78A-6-117(1)(b) are governed by this part.
(2)  School districts may enter into agreements with law enforcement agencies
for notification under Subsection (1).
