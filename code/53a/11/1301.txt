53A-11-1301.   Definitions.
(1)  The definitions in Sections 58-37-2, 58-37a-3, and 58-37b-2 apply to this
part.
(2)  As used in this part:
(a)  "Prohibited act" means an act punishable under Section 53A-3-501, Section
58-37-8, Section 58-37a-5, or Title 58, Chapter 37b.
(b)  "School" means a public or private elementary or secondary school.
