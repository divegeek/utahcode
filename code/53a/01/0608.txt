53A-1-608.   Preparation for tests.

(1)  School district employees may not carry on any specific instruction or preparation of students which would be a breach of testing ethics, such as the teaching of specific test questions.

(2)  School district employees who administer the test shall follow the standardization procedures in the publisher's test administration manual and any additional specific instructions developed by the State Board of Education.

(3)  The State Board of Education may revoke the certification of an individual who violates this section.



Enacted by Chapter 267, 1990 General Session
