53A-17a-116.   Weighted pupil units for career and technical education set-aside programs.

(1)  Each district shall receive a guaranteed minimum allocation from the money appropriated to the State Board of Education for a career and technical education set-aside program.

(2)  The set-aside funds remaining after the initial minimum payment allocation are distributed by an RFP process to help pay for equipment costs necessary to initiate new programs and for high priority programs as determined by labor market information.
