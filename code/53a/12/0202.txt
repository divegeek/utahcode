53A-12-202.   "Textbooks" defined.
For the purposes of Sections 53A-12-201 through 53A-12-206, "textbooks"
includes textbooks and workbooks necessary for participation in any
instructional course.  Textbooks shall not include personal or consumable
items, such as pencils, papers, pens, erasers, notebooks, other items of
personal use, or products which a student may purchase at his option, such as
school publications, class rings, annuals, and similar items.
