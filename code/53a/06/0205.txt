53A-6-205.   Effect of compact on other state laws and regulations.
(1)  Nothing in this compact shall be construed to repeal or otherwise modify
any law or regulation of a party state relating to the approval of programs of
educational preparation having effect solely on the qualification of
educational personnel within that state.
(2)  To the extent that contracts made pursuant to this compact deal with the
educational requirements for the proper qualification of educational
personnel, acceptance of a program of educational preparation shall be in
accordance with such procedures and requirements as may be provided in the
applicable contract.
