53A-11a-202.   Retaliation and making false allegation prohibited.
(1)  No school employee or student may engage in retaliation against:
(a)  a school employee;
(b)  a student; or
(c)  an investigator for, or a witness of, an alleged incident of bullying,
hazing, or retaliation.
(2)  No school employee or student may make a false allegation of bullying,
hazing, or retaliation against a school employee or student.
