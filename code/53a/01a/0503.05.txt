53A-1a-503.5.   Status of charter schools.
(1)  Charter schools are:
(a)  considered to be public schools within the state's public education
system;
(b)  subject to Subsection 53A-1-401(3); and
(c)  governed by independent boards and held accountable to a legally binding
written contractual agreement.
(2)  A charter school may be established by creating a new school or
converting an existing public school to charter status.
