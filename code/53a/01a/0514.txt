53A-1a-514.   Tort liability.
(1)  An employee of a charter school is a public employee and the governing
body is a public employer in the same manner as a local school board for
purposes of tort liability.
(2)  The governing body of a charter school, the nonprofit corporation under
which the charter school is organized and managed, and the school are solely
liable for any damages resulting from a legal challenge involving the
operation of the school.
