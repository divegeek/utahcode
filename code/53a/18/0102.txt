53A-18-102.   Additional indebtedness -- Election.
A local school board may require the qualified electors of the district to
vote on a proposition as to whether to incur indebtedness, subject to
conditions provided in Title 11, Chapter 14, Local Government Bonding Act,
under the following circumstances:
(1)  if the debts of the district are equal to school taxes and other
estimated revenues for the school year, and it is necessary to create and
incur additional indebtedness in order to maintain and support schools within
the district; or
(2)  the local school board determines it advisable to issue school district
bonds to purchase school sites, buildings, or furnishings or to improve
existing school property.
