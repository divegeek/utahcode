53A-25b-104.   Corporate powers.
(1)  The Utah Schools for the Deaf and the Blind is a public corporation with
perpetual succession and a corporate seal.
(2)  The Utah Schools for the Deaf and the Blind may:
(a)  sue and be sued;
(b)  contract and be contracted with;
(c)  take and hold by purchase, gift, devise, or bequest real and personal
property required for its uses; and
(d)  convert property, if not suitable for its use, into other property or
money.
(3)  The property of the Utah Schools for the Deaf and the Blind is exempt
from taxes and assessments.
