53A-16-103.   Duty of Division of Finance -- Apportionment of fund by state
board -- Certification of apportionments.
(1)  The Division of Finance shall give the state superintendent, upon
request, a written accounting of the current balance in the Uniform School
Fund.
(2)  The State Board of Education shall apportion the fund among the several
school districts.
(3)  The state superintendent shall certify the apportionments to the Division
of Finance and draws warrants on the state treasurer in favor of the school
districts.
