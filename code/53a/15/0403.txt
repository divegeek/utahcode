53A-15-403.   Local school boards' authority to direct adult education programs.

A local school board may do the following:

(1)  establish and maintain classes for adult education, with classes being held at times and places convenient and accessible to the members of the class;

(2)  raise and appropriate funds for an adult education program;

(3)  subject to Sections 53A-12-101 and 53A-15-401, determine fees for participation in an adult education program; and

(4)  hire persons to instruct adult education classes.
