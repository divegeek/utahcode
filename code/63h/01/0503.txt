63H-1-503.   Use of municipal services revenue.
(1)  The authority may use municipal services revenue to pay for:
(a)  administrative, overhead, legal, and other operating expenses of the
authority; and
(b)  municipal services within the project area from which the revenue was
collected.
(2)  Unless otherwise provided by agreement between the authority and each
county and municipality levying a property tax on property within a project
area, the authority shall distribute any remaining municipal services revenue
equally among all counties and municipalities that levy a property tax on
property within a project area.
