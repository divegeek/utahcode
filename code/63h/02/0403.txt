63H-2-403.   Purchaser of an authority bond.
(1)  The following may purchase an authority bond with funds owned or
controlled by the purchaser:
(a)  a person;
(b)  a political subdivision of the state;
(c)  another entity; or
(d)  a public or private officer.
(2)  Nothing in this section or Section 63H-2-402 may be construed to relieve
a purchaser of an authority bond of a duty to exercise reasonable care in
selecting securities.
(3)  The authority may purchase its own authority bond at a price that the
board determines.
