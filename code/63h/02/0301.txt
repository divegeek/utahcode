63H-2-301.   Prioritization of energy delivery projects.

(1)  The board shall review the location and availability of energy sources in the state.

(2)  The board shall:

(a)  determine if there is adequate infrastructure and capacity to bring energy sources to market;

(b)  prioritize projects on the basis of:

(i)  location;

(ii)  supporting responsible energy development;

(iii)  feasibility of development; and

(iv)  economic development factors; and

(c)  provide available funding to develop energy resource plans to provide for energy infrastructure development, including development of an integrated resource plan.
