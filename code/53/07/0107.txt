53-7-107.   Electronic writing.
(1)  Any writing required or permitted by this chapter may be filed or
prepared in an electronic medium and by electronic transmission subject to the
ability of the recipient to accept and process the electronic writing.
(2)  Any writing required by this chapter to be signed that is in an
electronic medium shall be signed by electronic signature in accordance with
Title 46, Chapter 4, Uniform Electronic Transactions Act.
