53-7-225.6.   Inspection and testing of fire alarm systems -- Certification
and exceptions.
(1) (a)  Each person, other than fire and building inspectors and electricians
licensed under Title 58, Chapter 55, Utah Construction Trades Licensing Act,
engaged in the inspection and testing of fire alarm systems shall be certified
by the state fire marshal.
(b)  The board shall by administrative rule prescribe:
(i)  an application form; and
(ii)  standards for certification qualification and for renewal and
revocation.
(2)  Applicants for certification as a fire alarm system inspector and tester
shall:
(a)  submit a written application on the form prescribed by the board;
(b)  provide evidence of competency as required by the board; and
(c)  submit the fee established under Subsection (3).
(3)  The board may establish an application fee under Section 63J-1-504.
