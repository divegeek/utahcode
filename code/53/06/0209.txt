53-6-209.   Termination of employment -- Change of status form.
(1)  When a peace officer's employment terminates, the employing agency shall
submit a change of status form noting the termination of the peace officer to
the division.
(2)  The change of status form shall:
(a)  be completed and submitted within seven days of the peace officer's
termination date;
(b)  identify the circumstances of the peace officer's status change by
indicating that the peace officer has resigned, retired, terminated,
transferred, deceased, or that the peace officer's name has changed;
(c)  indicate the effective date of action; and
(d)  indicate the name of the new employer, if the status change is due to a
transfer.
(3)  Any person or agency who intentionally falsifies, misrepresents, or fails
to give notice of the change of status of a peace officer is liable to the
division for any damages that may be sustained by the failure to make the
notification.
