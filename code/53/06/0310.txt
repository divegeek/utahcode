53-6-310.   Responsibility for training -- Certification.
(1)  The division is not responsible for providing basic or in-service
training for certified dispatchers except for approval of the instructors and
content of training where required by this chapter or division rules.
(2)  Where this chapter requires an agency head to certify that a member has
completed required training, the division shall rely on the certification, as
provided, to be accurate.
