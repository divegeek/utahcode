53-6-205.   Completion of training course required -- Persons affected.

(1) (a)  Except as provided in Subsection (2), before a person may be certified as a peace officer in Utah the person shall:

(i)  successfully complete the basic training course at a certified academy;

(ii)  pass the certification examination; and

(iii)  pass a physical fitness test.

(b)  A person may not exercise peace officer authority until certified.

(2)  Subsection (1) applies only to persons not previously certified and who receive their first employment appointment or election as a peace officer in Utah on or after January 1, 1985.
