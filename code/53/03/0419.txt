53-3-419.   Nonresident driver violations reported to resident state.
(1)  When the division receives a report of the conviction or plea in abeyance
of a nonresident holder of a CDL for a violation of a state law or local
ordinance relating to traffic control, the division shall notify the driver
licensing authority in the licensing state within ten days of receipt of the
report.
(2)  This section does not apply to parking violations.
