53-3-217.   License to be carried when driving motor vehicle -- Production
in court -- Violation.
(1) (a)  The licensee shall have his license certificate in his immediate
possession at all times when driving a motor vehicle.
(b)  A licensee shall display his license certificate upon demand of a justice
of peace, a peace officer, or a field deputy or inspector of the division.
(2)  It is a defense to a charge under this section that the person charged
produces in court a license certificate issued to him and valid at the time of
his citation or arrest.
(3)  A person who violates Subsection (1)(a) is guilty of a class C
misdemeanor.
