53-3-214.5.   License or identification card checkoff for vision screening.

(1)  A person who applies for a license or identification card or a renewal of a license or identification card may designate a voluntary contribution for vision screening of $2.

(2)  This contribution shall be:

(a)  collected by the division;

(b)  treated as a voluntary contribution to Friends For Sight to provide blindness prevention education, screening, and treatment and not as a license fee; and

(c)  transferred to Friends For Sight at least monthly, less actual administrative costs associated with collecting and transferring the contributions.
