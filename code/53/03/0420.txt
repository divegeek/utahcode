53-3-420.   Driver's driving record available for certain purposes.
The division shall provide full information regarding the driving record of
any holder of a CDL within 10 days of a request to:
(1)  the driver license administrator of any other state requesting that
information;
(2)  any employer or prospective employer of a person to drive a commercial
motor vehicle upon request and payment of a fee under Section 53-3-105;
(3)  insurers of commercial motor vehicle drivers upon request and payment of
a fee under Section 53-3-105; and
(4)  the Secretary of the United States Department of Transportation.
