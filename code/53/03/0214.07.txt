53-3-214.7.   License or identification card checkoff for promoting and
supporting organ donation.
(1)  A person who applies for a license or identification card or a renewal of
a license or identification card may designate a voluntary contribution of $2
for the purpose of promoting and supporting organ donation.
(2)  This contribution shall be:
(a)  collected by the division;
(b)  treated as a voluntary contribution to the Organ Donation Contribution
Fund created in Section 26-18b-101 and not as a license fee; and
(c)  transferred to the Organ Donation Contribution Fund created in Section
26-18b-101 at least monthly, less actual administrative costs associated with
collecting and transferring the contributions.
