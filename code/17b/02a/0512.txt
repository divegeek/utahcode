17B-2a-512.   Removal of land from the assessor's roll.
(1)  An irrigation district may direct a county treasurer to remove parcels of
land from the district's billing if:
(a)  the land is publicly dedicated to a street, highway, or road; or
(b)  the use of the land has so permanently changed as to prevent the
beneficial use of water on it.
(2)  Each county treasurer shall comply with the direction of an irrigation
district under Subsection (1).
