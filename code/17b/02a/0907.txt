17B-2a-907.   Adding a new service within a service area.

A service area may begin to provide within the boundaries of the service area a service that it had not previously provided by using the procedures set forth in Chapter 1, Part 2, Creation of a Local District, for the creation of a service area as though a new service area were being created to provide that service.
