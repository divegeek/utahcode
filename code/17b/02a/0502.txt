17B-2a-502.   Provisions applicable to irrigation districts.
(1)  Each irrigation district is governed by and has the powers stated in:
(a)  this part; and
(b)  Chapter 1, Provisions Applicable to All Local Districts.
(2)  This part applies only to irrigation districts.
(3)  An irrigation district is not subject to the provisions of any other part
of this chapter.
(4)  If there is a conflict between a provision in Chapter 1, Provisions
Applicable to All Local Districts, and a provision in this part, the provision
in this part governs.
