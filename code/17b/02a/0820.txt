17B-2a-820.   Authority for other governmental entities to acquire property
by eminent domain for a public transit district.
The state, a county, or a municipality may, by eminent domain under Title 78B,
Chapter 6, Part 5, Eminent Domain, acquire within its boundaries a private
property interest, including fee simple, easement, air right, right-of-way, or
other interest, necessary for the establishment or operation of a public
transit district.
