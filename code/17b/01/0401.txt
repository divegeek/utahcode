17B-1-401.   Definitions.

For purposes of this part:

(1)  "Applicable area" means:

(a)  for a county, the unincorporated area of the county that is included within the area proposed for annexation; or

(b)  for a municipality, the area of the municipality that is included within the area proposed for annexation.

(2)  "Retail" means, with respect to a service provided by a municipality or local district, that the service is provided directly to the ultimate user.

(3)  "Wholesale" means, with respect to a service provided by a local district, that the service is not provided directly to the ultimate user but is provided to a retail provider.
