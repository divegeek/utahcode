17B-1-418.   Annexed area subject to fees, charges, and taxes.

When an annexation under Section 17B-1-414 or 17B-1-415 or a boundary adjustment under Section 17B-1-417 is complete, the annexed area or the area affected by the boundary adjustment shall be subject to user fees or charges imposed by and property, sales, and other taxes levied by or for the benefit of the local district.
