20A-9-809.   Counting votes -- Canvass -- Certification of results to
parties.
(1)  Votes shall be counted, results tabulated, returns transmitted, ballots
reviewed and retained, returns canvassed, and recounts and election contests
conducted as provided in Title 20A, Chapter 4, Election Returns and Election
Contests.
(2)  After the canvass is complete and the report is prepared, the lieutenant
governor shall transmit a copy of the report to each registered political
party that participated in Utah's Western States Presidential Primary.
