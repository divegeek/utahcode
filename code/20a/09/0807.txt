20A-9-807.   Combining voting precincts.

(1)  The county legislative body may combine voting precincts for Utah's Western States Presidential Primary by following the procedures and requirements of Section 20A-5-303.

(2)  The county legislative body may not combine voting precincts if the voting precincts are in different congressional districts as established by Section 20A-13-102.



Enacted by Chapter 22, 1999 General Session
