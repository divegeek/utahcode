20A-2-300.5.   Definitions.
As used in this part:
(1)  "Discretionary voter registration agency" means each office designated by
the county clerk to provide by-mail voter registration forms to the public.
(2)  "Public assistance agency" means each office in Utah that provides:
(a)  public assistance; and
(b)  state funded programs primarily engaged in providing services to people
with disabilities.
