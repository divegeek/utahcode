20A-2-102.5.   Voter registration deadline.

(1)  Except as provided in Sections 20A-2-201 and 20A-2-206 and Chapter 16, Uniform Military and Overseas Voters Act, a person who fails to submit a correctly completed voter registration form on or before the voter registration deadline may not be permitted to vote in the election.

(2)  The voter registration deadline shall be the date that is 30 calendar days before the date of the election.
