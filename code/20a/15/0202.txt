20A-15-202.   Certificate of ratification.
(1)  If the convention agrees, by vote of a majority of the total number of
delegates, to ratify the proposed amendment, the president and secretary of
the convention shall:
(a)  prepare and sign a certificate to that effect; and
(b)  transmit it to the lieutenant governor.
(2)  Upon receipt of a ratification certificate, the lieutenant governor shall
transmit the certificate under the great seal of the state to the Secretary of
State of the United States.
