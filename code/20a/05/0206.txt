20A-5-206.   Change of precinct boundaries -- Revising list.

(1)  Whenever the boundaries of any voting precinct are changed, or a new voting precinct is created, the county clerk shall ensure that the names of all voters residing within the territory affected by  the change are transferred from one official register to the other.

(2)  Any registered voter whose name has been erroneously transferred from one official register to another, or erroneously allowed to remain on any official register, may vote in the voting precinct in which the voter resides if the voter uses a provisional ballot.



Amended by Chapter 251, 2012 General Session
