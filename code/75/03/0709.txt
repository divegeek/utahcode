75-3-709.   Power to avoid transfers.
The property liable for the payment of unsecured debts of a decedent includes
all property transferred by him by any means which is in law void or voidable
as against his creditors, and subject to prior liens, the right to recover
this property, so far as necessary for the payment of unsecured debts of the
decedent, is exclusively in the personal representative. The personal
representative is not required to institute such an action unless requested by
creditors who must pay or secure the cost and expenses of litigation.
