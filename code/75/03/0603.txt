75-3-603.   Bond not required -- Exceptions.
No bond is required of a personal representative appointed in formal or
informal proceedings, except (1) upon the appointment of a special
administrator without notice having been given, (2) when an executor or other
personal representative is appointed to administer an estate under a will
containing an express requirement of bond, (3) when bond is requested prior to
appointment, by an interested party, or (4) when bond is required under
Section 75-3-605. No bond is required of any personal representative who is
exempted from bond under Title 7. Bond required pursuant to this section may
be dispensed with upon a determination by the court that it is not necessary.
