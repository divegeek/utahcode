75-5a-121.   Termination of custodianship.

The custodian shall transfer in an appropriate manner the custodial property to the minor or to the minor's estate upon the earlier of:

(1)  the minor's becoming 21 years of age with respect to custodial property transferred under Section 75-5a-105 or 75-5a-106;

(2)  the minor's attainment of majority under the laws of this state with respect to the custodial property transferred under Section 75-5a-107 or 75-5a-108; or

(3)  the minor's death.



Enacted by Chapter 272, 1990 General Session
