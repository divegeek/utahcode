75-7-1008.   Exculpation of trustee.

A term of a trust relieving a trustee of liability for breach of trust is unenforceable to the extent that it:

(1)  relieves the trustee of liability for breach of trust committed in bad faith or with reckless indifference to the purposes of the trust or the interests of the beneficiaries; or

(2)  was inserted by the trustee or fiduciary without disclosure of its existence and contents.



Enacted by Chapter 89, 2004 General Session
