75-7-517.   Execution and levies prohibited.

(1)  No execution may issue upon nor may any levy be made against any property of the deceased settlor's estate under any judgment against a deceased settlor or a trustee.

(2)  This section may not be construed to prevent the enforcement of mortgages, pledges, or liens upon real or personal property in an appropriate proceeding.
