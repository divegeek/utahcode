75-5-422.   Sale, encumbrance or transaction involving conflict of interest
-- Voidable -- Exceptions.
Any sale or encumbrance to a conservator, his spouse, agent, or attorney, or
any corporation or trust in which he has a substantial beneficial interest, or
any transaction which is affected by a substantial conflict of interest, is
voidable unless the transaction is approved by the court after notice to
interested persons and others as directed by the court.
