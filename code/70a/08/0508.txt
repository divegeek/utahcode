70A-8-508.   Duty of securities intermediary to change entitlement holder's
position to other form of security holding.
A securities intermediary shall act at the direction of an entitlement holder
to change a security entitlement into another available form of holding for
which the entitlement holder is eligible, or to cause the financial asset to
be transferred to a securities account of the entitlement holder with another
securities intermediary.  A securities intermediary satisfies the duty if:
(1)  the securities intermediary acts as agreed upon by the entitlement holder
and the securities intermediary; or
(2)  in the absence of agreement, the securities intermediary exercises due
care in accordance with reasonable commercial standards to follow the
direction of the entitlement holder.
