70A-8-206.    Completion or alteration of security certificate.
(1)  If a security certificate contains the signatures necessary to its issue
or transfer but is incomplete in any other respect:
(a)  any person may complete it by filling in the blanks as authorized; and
(b)  even if the blanks are incorrectly filled in, the security certificate as
completed is enforceable by a purchaser who took it for value and without
notice of the incorrectness.
(2)  A complete security certificate that has been improperly altered, even if
fraudulently, remains enforceable, but only according to its original terms.
