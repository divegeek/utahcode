70A-4a-108.   Exclusion of consumer transactions governed by federal law.

This chapter does not apply to a funds transfer any part of which is governed by the Electronic Fund Transfer Act of 1978, Title XX, Public Law 95-630, 92 Stat.  3728, 15 U.S.C.  Section 1693 et seq., as amended.



Enacted by Chapter 294, 1990 General Session
