70A-2-712.   "Cover" -- Buyer's procurement of substitute goods.

(1)  After a breach within the preceding section the buyer may "cover" by
making in good faith and without unreasonable delay any reasonable purchase of
or contract to purchase goods in substitution for those due from the seller.
(2)  The buyer may recover from the seller as damages the difference between
the cost of cover and the contract price together with any incidental or
consequential damages as hereinafter defined (Section 70A-2-715), but less
expenses saved in consequence of the seller's breach.
(3)  Failure of the buyer to effect cover within this section does not bar him
from any other remedy.
