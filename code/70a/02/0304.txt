70A-2-304.   Price payable in money, goods, realty, or otherwise.

(1)  The price can be made payable in money or otherwise. If it is payable in
whole or in part in goods each party is a seller of the goods which he is to
transfer.
(2)  Even though all or part of the price is payable in an interest in realty
the transfer of the goods and the seller's obligations with reference to them
are subject to this chapter, but not the transfer of the interest in realty or
the transferor's obligations in connection therewith.
