70A-7a-701.   Applicability.

This chapter applies to a document of title that is issued or a bailment that arises on or after May 1, 2006.  This chapter does not apply to a document of title that is issued or a bailment that arises before May 1, 2006 even if the document of title or bailment would be subject to this chapter if the document of title had been issued or bailment had arisen on or after May 1, 2006.  This chapter does not apply to a right of action that has accrued before May 1, 2006.



Enacted by Chapter 42, 2006 General Session
