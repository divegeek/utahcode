70A-9a-708.   Persons entitled to file initial financing statement or
continuation statement.
A person may file an initial financing statement or a continuation statement
under this part if:
(1)  the secured party of record authorizes the filing; and
(2)  the filing is necessary under this part:
(a)  to continue the effectiveness of a financing statement filed before this
act takes effect; or
(b)  to perfect or continue the perfection of a security interest.
