70A-9a-342.    Bank's right to refuse to enter into or disclose existence of control agreement.

This chapter does not require a bank to enter into an agreement of the kind described in Subsection 70A-9a-104(1)(b), even if its customer so requests or directs.  A bank that has entered into such an agreement is not required to confirm the existence of the agreement to another person unless requested to do so by its customer.
