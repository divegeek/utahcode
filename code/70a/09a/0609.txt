70A-9a-609.   Secured party's right to take possession after default.
(1)  After default, a secured party:
(a)  may take possession of the collateral; and
(b)  without removal, may render equipment unusable and dispose of collateral
on a debtor's premises under Section 70A-9a-610.
(2)  A secured party may proceed under Subsection (1):
(a)  pursuant to judicial process; or
(b)  without judicial process, if it proceeds without breach of the peace.
(3) If so agreed, and in any event after default, a secured party may require
the debtor to assemble the collateral and make it available to the secured
party at a place to be designated by the secured party which is reasonably
convenient to both parties.
