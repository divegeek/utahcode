70A-9a-808.   Priority.

Laws of Utah 2013, Chapter 225 determines the priority of conflicting claims to collateral.  However, if the relative priorities of the claims were established before Laws of Utah 2013, Chapter 225 takes effect, this chapter as it existed before amendment determines priority.
