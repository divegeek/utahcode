70A-9a-209.    Duties of secured party if account debtor has been notified
of assignment.
(1)  Except as otherwise provided in Subsection (3), this section applies if:
(a)  there is no outstanding secured obligation; and
(b)  the secured party is not committed to make advances, incur obligations,
or otherwise give value.
(2)  Within 10 days after receiving an authenticated demand by the debtor, a
secured party shall send to an account debtor that has received notification
of an assignment to the secured party as assignee under Subsection 70A-
9a-406(1) an authenticated record that releases the account debtor from any
further obligation to the secured party.
(3)  This section does not apply to an assignment constituting the sale of an
account, chattel paper, or payment intangible.
