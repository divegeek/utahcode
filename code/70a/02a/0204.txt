70A-2a-204.   Formation in general.
(1)  A lease contract may be made in any manner sufficient to show agreement,
including conduct by both parties which recognizes the existence of a lease
contract.
(2)  An agreement sufficient to constitute a lease contract may be found
although the moment of its making is undetermined.
(3)  Although one or more terms are left open, a lease contract does not fail
for indefiniteness if the parties have intended to make a lease contract and
there is a reasonably certain basis for giving an appropriate remedy.
