70A-2a-522.   Lessee's right to goods on lessor's insolvency.
(1)  Subject to Subsection (2) and even though the goods have not been
shipped, a lessee who has paid a part or all of the rent and security for
goods identified to a lease contract as provided in Section 70A-2a-217 on
making and keeping good a tender of any unpaid portion of the rent and
security due under the lease contract may recover the goods identified from
the lessor if the lessor becomes insolvent within 10 days after receipt of the
first installment of rent and security.
(2)  A lessee acquires the right to recover goods identified to a lease
contract only if they conform to the lease contract.
