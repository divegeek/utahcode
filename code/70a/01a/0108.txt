70A-1a-108.   Relation to electronic signatures in global and national
commerce act.
This chapter modifies, limits, and supersedes the federal Electronic
Signatures in Global and National Commerce Act, 15 U.S.C. Section 7001 et
seq., except that nothing in this chapter modifies, limits, or supersedes
Section 7001(c) of that act or authorizes electronic delivery of any of the
notices described in Section 7003(b) of that act.
