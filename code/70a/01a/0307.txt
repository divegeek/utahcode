70A-1a-307.   Prima facie evidence by third-party documents.
A document in due form purporting to be a bill of lading, policy or
certificate of insurance, official weigher's or inspector's certificate,
consular invoice, or any other document authorized or required by the contract
to be issued by a third party is prima facie evidence of its own authenticity
and genuineness and of the facts stated in the document by the third party.
