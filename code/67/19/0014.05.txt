67-19-14.5.   Organ donor leave.
(1)  An employee who serves as a bone marrow donor shall be granted a paid
leave of absence of up to seven days that are necessary for the donation and
recovery from the donation.
(2)  An employee who serves as a donor of a human organ shall be granted a
paid leave of absence of up to 30 days that are necessary for the donation and
recovery from the donation.
