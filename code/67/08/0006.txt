67-8-6.   Legislative fiscal analyst performing administrative functions for commission -- Employment of professional assistance -- Assistance of state agencies -- Publication of reports.

Administration, budgeting, procurement, and related management functions for the commission shall be performed by the legislative fiscal analyst. The commission is, however, authorized to employ professional assistance if deemed necessary and to request assistance from any agency or institution of the state. It may also prepare, publish, and distribute from time to time reports of its studies and recommendations and statements in support of its recommendations.



Enacted by Chapter 267, 1981 General Session
