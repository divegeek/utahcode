67-5-1.5.   Special duties -- Employment of staff.

(1)  The attorney general may undertake special duties and projects as follows:

(a)  employment of child protection services investigators under Section 67-5-16;

(b)  employment of an Obscenity and Pornography Complaints Ombudsman under Section 67-5-18;

(c)  administration of the Internet Crimes Against Children Task Force under Section 67-5-20;

(d)  administration of the Internet Crimes Against Children (ICAC) Unit under Section 67-5-21;

(e)  administration of the Identity Theft Reporting Information System (IRIS) Program under Section 67-5-22;

(f)  administration of the Attorney General Crime and Violence Prevention Fund under Section 67-5-24;

(g)  administration of the Safety Net Initiative as provided under Section 67-5-26; and

(h)  administration of the Mortgage and Financial Fraud Unit under Section 67-5-30.

(2)  As permitted by the provisions of this chapter, the attorney general may employ or contract with investigators, prosecutors, and necessary support staff to fulfill the special duties undertaken under this section.
