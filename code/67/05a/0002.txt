67-5a-2.   Terms -- Filling vacancies -- Chair.
(1)  The term of each council member is four years, unless the term is earlier
terminated by:
(a)  the authority that designated the member; or
(b)  the member ceasing to hold the office that qualified him for membership.
(2)  A member whose term has expired may continue, for not more than four
months, to serve as a council member until a successor is selected and
qualified.
(3)  Council members may serve for more than one successive term.
(4)  When a vacancy occurs in the membership for any reason, the replacement
shall be appointed for a full term that commences on the date of appointment.
(5)  The council shall elect by a majority vote one of its members as chair at
its first meeting and then annually.
