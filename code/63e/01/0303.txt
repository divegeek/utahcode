63E-1-303.   Recommendations of the committee.
After the committee has reviewed a proposal to create an independent entity in
accordance with Section 63E-1-302, the committee shall make a report to the
Legislature stating whether the committee:
(1)  recommends creation of the proposed independent entity;
(2)  recommends that the proposed independent entity not be created; or
(3)  makes no recommendation regarding the creation of the proposed
independent entity.
