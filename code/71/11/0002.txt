71-11-2.   Definitions.

As used in this chapter:

(1)  "Administrator" means a Veterans' Nursing Home Administrator selected in accordance with Section 71-11-5.

(2)  "Board" means any Veterans' Nursing Home Advisory Board.

(3)  "Department" means the Department of Veterans' Affairs created in Section 71-8-2.

(4)  "Executive director" means the executive director of the Department of Veterans' Affairs.

(5)  "Home" means any Utah Veterans' Nursing Home.

(6)  "Veteran" is as defined in Subsection 71-10-1(4).
