4-9-4.   Weights and measures -- Primary state standards -- Secondary state
standards -- Verification.
(1)  Weights and measures that are traceable to the United States prototype
standards supplied by the federal government, or approved as being
satisfactory by the National Institute of Standards and Technology, shall be
the state primary standards, and shall be maintained in the calibration
prescribed by the National Institute of Standards and Technology.
(2)  Secondary standards may be prescribed by the department and shall be
verified upon their initial receipt, and as often after initial receipt as is
considered necessary by the department.
