4-9-11.   Advertisement of packaged commodity sales -- Requirements.

An advertisement which promotes a packaged commodity with the retail price stated shall plainly and conspicuously advertise the quantity required to appear on the package.  If a dual quantity declaration is required by law, only the declaration that sets forth the quantity in terms of the smaller unit of weight or measure need appear in the advertisement.
