4-37-303.   Transportation of live aquatic animals to fee fishing
facilities.
(1)  Any person holding a certificate of registration for a fee fishing
facility may transport the live aquatic animals specified on the certificate
of registration to the facility.
(2)  Each transfer or shipment of live aquatic animals to a fee fishing
facility within the state must be accompanied by documentation of the source
and destination of the fish, including:
(a)  name, address, certificate of registration number and health approval
number of the source;
(b)  number and weight being shipped by species; and
(c)  name, address, and certificate of registration number of the destination.
