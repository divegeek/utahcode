4-16-6.   Chapter does not apply to seed not intended for sowing, to seed at
seed processing plant, or to seed transported or delivered for transportation
in the ordinary course of business.
This chapter does not apply to:
(1)  seed or grain not intended for sowing;
(2)  seed at, or consigned to, a seed processing or cleaning plant; provided,
that any label or any other representation which is made with respect to the
uncleaned or unprocessed seed is subject to this chapter; or
(3)  to any carrier in respect to any seed transported or delivered for
transportation in the ordinary course of its business as a carrier; provided,
the carrier is not engaged in producing, processing, or marketing
agricultural, vegetable, flower, or tree and shrub seeds or seeds for
sprouting.
