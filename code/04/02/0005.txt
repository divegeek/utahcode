4-2-5.   Submission of department's budget.
The commissioner, on or before October 1 of each year, shall submit an
itemized budget for the department to the governor. The proposed budget shall
contain a complete plan of proposed expenditures and estimated revenues for
the ensuing fiscal year and shall be accompanied by a statement setting forth
the revenues and expenditures for the fiscal year next preceding, and the
current assets and liabilities of the department, including restricted revenue
accounts and dedicated credits.
