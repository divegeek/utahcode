4-19-3.   Loans -- Not to exceed period of 10 years -- Agricultural Advisory
Board to approve loans and renewals, methods of payments, and interest rates
-- Guidelines in fixing interest rates declared.
(1)  The department may not make a loan authorized under this chapter for a
period to exceed 10 years but the loan is renewable.
(2)  The Agricultural Advisory Board shall approve:
(a)  all loans and renewals;
(b)  the methods of repayment; and
(c)  the interest rates charged.
(3)  In fixing interest rates, the Agricultural Advisory Board shall consider:
(a)  the current applicable interest rate or rates being charged by the USDA
Farm Service Agency on similar loans;
(b)  the current prime rate charged by leading lending institutions; and
(c)  any other pertinent economic data.
(4)  The interest rates established shall be compatible with guidelines stated
in this section.
