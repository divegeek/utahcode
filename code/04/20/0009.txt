4-20-9.   Commissioner to supervise distribution of undistributed funds if United States alters or discontinues funding leaving funds or resources available.

If the United States alters or discontinues funding under the Taylor Grazing Act or the operation of advisory boards, leaving funds or other resources undistributed or otherwise without means for continuation, the commissioner shall supervise and control the distribution of such undistributed funds or other resources.



Enacted by Chapter 2, 1979 General Session
