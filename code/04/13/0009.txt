4-13-9.   Sales or exchanges of commercial fertilizers or soil amendments between manufacturers, importers, or manipulators permitted.

Nothing in this chapter shall be construed to restrict or avoid sales or exchanges of commercial fertilizers or soil amendments to each other by importers, manufacturers, or manipulators who mix fertilizer or soil amendment materials for sale or as preventing the free and unrestricted shipment of commercial fertilizer or soil amendments to manufacturers or manipulators who have registered their brands as required by this chapter.



Enacted by Chapter 2, 1979 General Session
