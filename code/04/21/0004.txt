4-21-4.   Refund of fees allowed -- Claim for refund to be filed with
department -- Payment of refunds.
A person who objects to payment of the assessed fee may file a claim with the
department within 60 days after the fee is collected.  No claim for refund,
however, is allowed if it is filed more than 60 days after the date the fee is
collected.  Each claim for refund shall be certified by the department to the
state treasurer for payment from the beef promotion account, subject to any
applicable provisions of the Beef Promotion and Research Act of 1985, 7 U.S.C.
Sec.  2901 et seq.
