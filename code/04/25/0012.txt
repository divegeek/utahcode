4-25-12.   Allowing swine to run at large -- Class B misdemeanor.

(1)  A person is guilty of a class B misdemeanor if the person:

(a)  is in control of a swine; and

(b)  allows the swine to run at large.

(2)  A person described in Subsection (1) is liable for damage caused by the swine running at large.
