55-5a-5.   Application for permit -- Investigation -- Exception -- Appeal of denial.

The division shall investigate each application for a permit to assure that the person, group of persons or organization is actually engaged in the manufacture or distribution of goods, articles or products made by blind persons within the meaning of this act. The division may issue permits without investigation, however, to nonresident persons, groups of persons or organizations upon proof that they are recognized and approved by the state in which they reside as authorized to sell such goods, articles or products pursuant to a law of that state imposing requirements substantially similar to those prescribed pursuant to this act.

Anyone denied a permit may appeal the decision of the division to the state superintendent of public instruction or his designated agent.
