55-5-8.   Food service in exempt buildings.
With respect to all state, county, and municipal buildings which are not
subject to Section 55-5-7, the governmental agency in charge of the building
shall consider allowing the Division of Services for the Blind and Visually
Impaired to operate any existing or proposed food service in the building, and
shall discuss the operation with the division under Section 53A-24-304 upon
its request.
