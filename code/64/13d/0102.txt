64-13d-102.   Definitions.

(1)  As used in this chapter:

(a)  "Contractor" means a private entity seeking to contract with or currently contracting with the department to establish or operate a private correctional facility.

(b)  "Correctional services" means those services necessary for the operation of a correctional facility, including the provision of food, clothing, security, programs, and health care.

(c)  "Facility" means a private correctional facility established or operated under a contract with the department.

(2)  The definitions used under Section 64-13-1 apply to this chapter.
