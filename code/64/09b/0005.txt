64-9b-5.   Intent of Legislature.

It is the legislative intent, and inmates are encouraged, to use their personal earnings from jobs created under this chapter for the following:

(1)  for restitution to the victims of the inmate's criminal offense, where applicable;

(2)  for support of the inmate's family, where applicable;

(3)  for the inmate's personal use; and

(4)  for reimbursement of security, operational, and other costs incurred by the Utah Correctional Industries Division of the department in administering these projects.



Amended by Chapter 158, 1997 General Session
