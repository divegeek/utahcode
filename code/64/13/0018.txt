64-13-18.   Copy of sentence of incarceration.

The officer delivering any offender for incarceration shall deliver to the department a certified copy of the sentence received by the officer from the clerk of the court.  The department shall give the officer a certificate of delivery and shall submit to the Board of Pardons and Parole a copy of the commitment order.  The certified copy of sentence is conclusive evidence of the facts contained in it.
