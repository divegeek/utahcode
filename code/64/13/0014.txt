64-13-14.   Secure correctional facilities.
(1)  The department shall maintain and operate secure correctional facilities
for the incarceration of offenders.
(2)  For each compound of secure correctional facilities, as established by
the executive director, wardens shall be appointed as the chief administrative
officers by the executive director.
(3)  The department may transfer offenders from one correctional facility to
another and may, with the consent of the sheriff, transfer any offender to a
county jail.
