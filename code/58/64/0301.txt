58-64-301.   Licensure required -- License classifications.

(1)  A license is required to engage in the practice of deception detection, except as specifically provided in Section 58-64-304 or 58-1-307.

(2)  The division shall issue to an individual who qualifies under this chapter a license in the classifications of:

(a)  deception detection examiner; or

(b)  deception detection intern.



Enacted by Chapter 215, 1995 General Session
