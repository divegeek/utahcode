58-5a-307.   Consumer access to provider charges.
Beginning January 1, 2011, a podiatric physician licensed under this chapter
shall, when requested by a consumer:
(1)  make a list of professional charges available for the consumer which
includes the podiatric physician's 25 most frequently performed:
(a)  clinical procedures or clinical services;
(b)  out-patient procedures; and
(c)  in-patient procedures; and
(2)  provide the consumer with information regarding any discount available
for:
(a)  services not covered by insurance; or
(b)  prompt payment of billed charges.
