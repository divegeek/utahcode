58-17b-603.   Identification of pharmacy personnel.

(1)  All individuals employed in a pharmacy facility having any contact with the public or patients receiving services from that pharmacy facility shall wear on their person a clearly visible and readable identification showing the individual's name and position.

(2)  When communicating by any means, written, verbal, or electronic, pharmacy personnel must identify themselves as to licensure classification.
