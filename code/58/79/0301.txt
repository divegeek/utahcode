58-79-301.   Licensure required -- Issuance of licenses.
(1)  Beginning January 1, 2010, and except as provided in Sections 58-1-307
and 58-79-304, a license is required to provide the services of a hunting
guide or outfitter.
(2)  The division shall issue to an individual who qualifies under the
provisions of this chapter a license in the classification of:
(a)  hunting guide; or
(b)  outfitter.
