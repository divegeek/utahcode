58-76-301.   License required -- License classification.

(1)  After January 1, 2003, a license is required to engage in the practice of geology before the public except as specifically provided in Sections 58-1-307 and 58-76-304.

(2)  The division shall issue to a person who qualifies under the provisions of this chapter a license in the classification of professional geologist.



Enacted by Chapter 218, 2002 General Session
