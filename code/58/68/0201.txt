58-68-201.   Board.
(1)  There is created the Osteopathic Physician and Surgeon’s Licensing
Board consisting of four osteopathic physicians and surgeons and one member of
the general public.
(2)  The board shall be appointed and serve in accordance with Section
58-1-201.
(3) (a)  The duties and responsibilities of the board are in accordance with
Sections 58-1-202 and 58-1-203.
(b)  The board may also designate one of its members on a permanent or
rotating basis to:
(i)  assist the division in reviewing complaints concerning the unlawful or
unprofessional conduct of a licensee; and
(ii)  advise the division in its investigation of these complaints.
(4)  A board member who has, under Subsection (3), reviewed a complaint or
advised in its investigation may be disqualified from participating with the
board when the board serves as a presiding officer in an adjudicative
proceeding concerning that complaint.
