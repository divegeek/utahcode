58-68-502.   Unprofessional conduct.

"Unprofessional conduct" includes, in addition to the definition in Section 58-1-501:

(1)  using or employing the services of any individual to assist a licensee in any manner not in accordance with the generally recognized practices, standards, or ethics of the profession, state law, or division rule; or

(2)  violating the dispensing requirements of Section 58-17b-309 or 58-17b-309.5 if applicable.
