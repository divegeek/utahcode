58-74-501.   Unlawful conduct.

(1)  It is unlawful for any person not licensed in accordance with this chapter to assume the title or use the abbreviation C.S.R. or C.V.R. or any other similar words, letters, figures, or abbreviation to indicate that the person using that title or abbreviation is a certified court reporter.

(2)  Violation of this provision is a class A misdemeanor.
