58-72-502.   Penalty for unlawful conduct.
(1)  Any person who violates the unlawful conduct provision defined in
Subsection 58-1-501(1)(a) is guilty of a third degree felony.
(2)  Any person who violates any of the unlawful conduct provisions defined in
Subsections 58-1-501(1)(b) through (e) is guilty of a class A misdemeanor.
