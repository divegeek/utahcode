58-28-503.   Penalty for unlawful or unprofessional conduct.
(1)  Any person who violates the unlawful conduct provisions of Section
58-28-501 is guilty of a third degree felony.
(2)  After proceeding pursuant to Title 63G, Chapter 4, Administrative
Procedures Act, and Chapter 1, Division of Occupational and Professional
Licensing Act, the division may impose administrative penalties of up to
$10,000 for acts of unprofessional conduct or unlawful conduct under this
chapter.
(3)  Assessment of a penalty under this section does not affect any other
action the division is authorized to take regarding a license issued under
this chapter.
