58-78-501.   Unlawful conduct.

In addition to the definition in Section 58-1-501, "unlawful conduct" includes using the title "vocational rehabilitation counselor" or any other title or designation tending to indicate that the individual is a vocational rehabilitation counselor unless the individual has a current license as a vocational rehabilitation counselor issued under this chapter.



Enacted by Chapter 122, 2009 General Session
