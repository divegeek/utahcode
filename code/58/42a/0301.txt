58-42a-301.   Licensure required -- License classification.

(1)  A license is required to engage in the practice of occupational therapy, except as specifically provided in Section 58-1-307 or 58-42a-304.

(2)  The division shall issue to a person who qualifies under this chapter a license in the classification of:

(a)  occupational therapist; or

(b)  occupational therapy assistant.

(3)  Nothing in this chapter shall permit an individual licensed under this chapter to engage in the practice of mental health therapy.
