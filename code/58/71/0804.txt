58-71-804.   Insurance coverage not mandated.

(1)  This chapter does not mandate health insurance coverage for naturopathic medical services.

(2)  This chapter does not establish a class of health care providers for the purposes of Section 31A-22-618.

(3)  This chapter does not mandate health insurance coverage for the prescription or administration of testosterone, as described in Subsection 58-71-102(8)(e), by a naturopathic physician.
