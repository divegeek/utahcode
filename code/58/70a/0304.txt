58-70a-304.   License renewal -- Continuing education.

(1)  Prior to license renewal, each licensee shall, during each two-year licensure cycle or other cycle defined by division rule, complete qualified continuing professional education requirements as defined by division rule made in collaboration with the board.

(2)  If a renewal period is extended or shortened under Section 58-70a-303, the continuing education hours required for license renewal under this section are increased or decreased proportionally.
