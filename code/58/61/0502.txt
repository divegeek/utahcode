58-61-502.   Unprofessional conduct.
(1)  As used in this chapter, "unprofessional conduct" includes:
(a)  using or employing the services of any individual to assist a licensee in
any manner not in accordance with the generally recognized practices,
standards, or ethics of the profession for which the individual is licensed,
or the laws of the state;
(b)  failure to confine practice conduct to those acts or practices:
(i)  in which the individual is competent by education, training, and
experience within limits of education, training, and experience; and
(ii)  which are within applicable scope of practice laws of this chapter; and
(c)  disclosing or refusing to disclose any confidential communication under
Section 58-61-602.
(2)  "Unprofessional conduct" under this chapter may be further defined by
division rule.
