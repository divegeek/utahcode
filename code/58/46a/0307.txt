58-46a-307.   Licensee required to prove licensure to commence or maintain
action.
A person may not commence or maintain an action in any court of the state for
collection or compensation in any amount resulting from performance of acts or
practices for which a license is required under this chapter unless that
person was properly licensed under this chapter as a hearing instrument
specialist at the time there was an agreement made to perform the regulated
acts and practices, when the regulated acts and practices were performed, and
when the alleged cause of action arose.
