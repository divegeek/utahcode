58-31b-304.   Qualifications for admission to the examinations.
(1)  To be admitted to the examinations required for certification as a
medication aide certified, a person shall:
(a)  submit an application on a form prescribed by the division;
(b)  pay a fee as determined by the division under Section 63J-1-504; and
(c)  meet all requirements of Subsection 58-31b-302(1), except the passing of
the examination.
(2)  To be admitted to the examinations required for licensure as a practical
nurse, a person shall:
(a)  submit an application form prescribed by the division;
(b)  pay a fee as determined by the division under Section 63J-1-504; and
(c)  meet all requirements of Subsection 58-31b-302(2), except Subsection
(2)(f).
(3)  To be admitted to the examinations required for licensure as a registered
nurse, a person shall:
(a)  submit an application form prescribed by the division;
(b)  pay a fee as determined by the division under Section 63J-1-504; and
(c)  meet all the requirements of Subsection 58-31b-302(3), except Subsection
(3)(f).
