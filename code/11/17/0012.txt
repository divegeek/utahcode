11-17-12.   Bonds -- Eligibility as investments and for use as security.
Bonds issued under this act are hereby made securities in which all public
officers and public bodies of the state and its political subdivisions, all
insurance companies, credit unions, building and loan associations, trust
companies, banking associations, investment companies, executors,
administrators, trustees and other fiduciaries, pension, profit-sharing and
retirement funds may properly and legally invest funds, including capital in
their control or belonging to them.  Such bonds are hereby made securities
which may properly and legally be deposited with and received by any state or
municipal officer or any agency or political subdivision of the state for any
purpose for which the deposit of bonds or obligations of the state is now or
may hereafter be authorized by law.
