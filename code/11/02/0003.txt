11-2-3.   Recreation board.
Authority to supervise and maintain any of such recreational facilities and
activities may be vested in any existing body or board, or in a public
recreation board, as the governing body of any city, town, county or school
district may determine. If it is determined that such powers are to be
exercised by a public recreation board, such board may be established in any
city, town, county or school district and shall possess all the powers and be
subject to all the responsibilities of the respective local authorities under
this chapter.
