11-13-102.   Purpose of chapter.
The purpose of this chapter is:
(1)  to permit local governmental units to make the most efficient use of
their powers by enabling them to cooperate with other localities on a basis of
mutual advantage and thereby to provide services and facilities in a manner
and under forms of governmental organization that will accord best with
geographic, economic, population and other factors influencing the needs and
development of local communities; and
(2) to provide the benefit of economy of scale, economic development, and
utilization of natural resources for the overall promotion of the general
welfare of the state.
