11-14-402.   Exemptions from application of chapter -- Exception.
(1)  Except as provided in Subsection (2), this chapter does not apply to
bonds issued by the state of Utah nor to bonds or obligations payable solely
from special assessments levied on benefited property.
(2)  Sections 11-14-303 and 11-14-501 have general application in accordance
with their terms.
