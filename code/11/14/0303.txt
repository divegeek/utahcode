11-14-303.   Bonds, notes, or other obligations of political subdivisions
exempt from taxation except corporate franchise tax.
All bonds, notes, or other obligations issued under this chapter or under any
other law authorizing the issuance of bonds, notes, or indebtedness by a local
political subdivision or any other political subdivision now existing or
subsequently created under the laws of Utah, including bonds payable solely
from special assessments and tax anticipation indebtedness, and the interest
on them shall be exempt from all taxation in this state, except for the
corporate franchise tax.
