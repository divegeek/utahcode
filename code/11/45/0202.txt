11-45-202.   Criteria for loans.

(1)  The office shall make a loan from the fund to a political subdivision only to finance an energy efficiency project.

(2)  The office may not make a loan from the fund:

(a)  to finance a political subdivision's compliance with the energy code in the construction of a new building; or

(b)  with a term of less than two years or more than 12 years.
