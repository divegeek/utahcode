11-4-2.   Duty of local governing body -- Maintenance of existing equipment.

(1)  Each local governing body installing any completely new fire protection system shall comply with Title 53, Chapter 7, Utah Fire Prevention and Safety Act.

(2)  A local governing body operating fire protection equipment may maintain, repair, replace, and extend  the equipment with equipment of like character and standards.
