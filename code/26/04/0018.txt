26-4-18.   Records of medical examiner -- Admissibility as evidence --
Subpoena of person who prepared record.
The records of the medical examiner or transcripts thereof certified by the
medical examiner are admissible as evidence in any civil action in any court
in this state except that statements by witnesses or other persons, unless
taken pursuant to Section 26-4-21, as conclusions upon extraneous matters are
not hereby made admissible. The person who prepared a report or record offered
in evidence hereunder may be subpoenaed as a witness in the case by any party.
