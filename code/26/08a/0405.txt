26-8a-405.   Ground ambulance and paramedic licenses -- Agency notice of
approval.
(1)  Beginning January 1, 2004, if the department determines that the
application meets the minimum requirements for licensure under Section
26-8a-404, the department shall issue a notice of the approved application to
the applicant.
(2)  A current license holder responding to a request for proposal under
Section 26-8a-405.2 is considered an approved applicant for purposes of
Section 26-8a-405.2 if the current license holder, prior to responding to the
request for proposal, submits the following to the department:
(a)  the information required by Subsection 26-8a-404(4)(a)(ii); and
(b)  if the license holder is a private entity, a financial statement, a pro
forma budget and necessary letters of credit demonstrating a financial ability
to expand service to a new service area; or
(c)  if the license holder is a governmental entity, a letter from the
governmental entity's governing body demonstrating the governing body's
willingness to financially support the application.
