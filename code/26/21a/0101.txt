26-21a-101.   Definitions.
As used in this chapter:
(1)  "Breast cancer screening mammography" means a standard two-view per
breast, low-dose as defined by the National Cancer Institute, radiographic
examination of the breasts to detect unsuspected breast cancer using equipment
designed and dedicated specifically for mammography.
(2)  "Diagnostic mammography" means mammography performed on a woman having
suspected breast cancer.
(3)  "Facility" means a facility that provides screening or diagnostic breast
mammography services.
