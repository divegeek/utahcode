35A-5-102.   Federal grants for retraining.
(1)  By following the procedures and requirements of Title 63J, Chapter 5,
Federal Funds Procedures, the state, through the Employment Development
Division may and is encouraged to apply for retraining, community assistance,
or technology transfer funds available through:
(a)  the United States Department of Defense;
(b)  United States Department of Labor; or
(c)  other appropriate federal offices or departments.
(2)  In applying for federal funds, the state through its Employment
Development Division or other appropriate office may inform the federal
government of state matching or enhancement funds if those funds are available
under Section 67-1-12.
