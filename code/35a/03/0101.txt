35A-3-101.   Title -- Employment assistance.

(1)  This chapter shall be known as the "Employment Support Act."

(2)  A person eligible for employment assistance under Chapter 3 or 5 shall receive any assistance under the applicable chapter, including stabilization, assessment, training, or placement, through the department in accordance with Chapter 2, Part 2, Service Delivery.
