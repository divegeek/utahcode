35A-3-110.   Third party obligation -- Interest.
Whenever the division expends public assistance on behalf of a client for
services or supplies, for which another person is obligated to reimburse the
division, that other person shall make such reimbursement within 60 days of
notification by the division.  If reimbursement is not made within that
period, and no extension of time is granted by the division, interest shall
accrue on the unpaid balance at the rate of 8% per annum.
