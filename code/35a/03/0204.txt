35A-3-204.   Duties of director.

The director shall:

(1)  enforce rules made by the department regulating the use of services provided by the office;

(2)  supervise office staff and prepare an annual work plan; and

(3)  apply for, accept, and expend gifts or donations from public or private sources to assist the office in fulfilling its statutory obligations.
