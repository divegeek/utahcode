68-3-1.   Common law adopted.
The common law of England so far as it is not repugnant to, or in conflict
with, the constitution or laws of the United States, or the constitution or
laws of this state, and so far only as it is consistent with and adapted to
the natural and physical conditions of this state and the necessities of the
people hereof, is hereby adopted, and shall be the rule of decision in all
courts of this state.
