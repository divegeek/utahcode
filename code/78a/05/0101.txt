78A-5-101.   State District Court Administrative System.

(1)  The district court is a trial court of general jurisdiction.

(2)  There is established a State District Court Administrative System.  The Judicial Council shall administer the operation of the system.

(3)  In this chapter, "court system" means the State District Court Administrative System.

(4)  A district court shall be located in the county seat of each county.
