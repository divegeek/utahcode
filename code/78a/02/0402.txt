78A-2-402.   Definitions.
As used in this part:
(1)  "Certified court reporter" has the same meaning as in Title 58, Chapter
74, Certified Court Reporters Licensing Act.
(2)  "Folio" means 100 words.  A number expressed as a numeral counts as one
word; however, any portion of the last folio is not counted.
(3)  "Official court reporter" means a certified court reporter employed by
the courts.
(4)  "Official court transcriber" means a person certified in accordance with
rules of the Judicial Council as competent to transcribe into written form an
audio or video recording of court proceedings.
