78A-2-221.   Justices and judges -- Limitations during terms.
A justice or judge of any court of record may not, during his term of office:
(1)  practice law or have a partner engaged in the practice of law;
(2)  hold office in or make any contribution to any political party or
organization engaged in political activity; or
(3)  use, in his efforts to obtain or retain judicial office, any political
party designation, reference, or description.
