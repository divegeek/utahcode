78A-11-113.   Subpoena power of the commission -- Testimony -- Contempt.
(1)  The commission may issue subpoenas in aid of an investigation of a
complaint filed with the commission.  The subpoena shall have the same
authority as an order of the district court.  Commission subpoenas shall be
issued in the manner and form prescribed for subpoenas by the Utah Rules of
Civil Procedure.  Commission subpoenas shall be served in the manner
prescribed for subpoenas by the Utah Rules of Civil Procedure.
(2)  The commission may administer oaths and compel testimony under oath in
aid of an investigation of a complaint filed with the commission and at
hearings before the commission.
(3)  If a person fails to comply with a subpoena, or if a person appears
before the commission and refuses to testify to a matter upon which the person
may be lawfully questioned, the person is in contempt of the commission, and
the commission may file in the district court a motion for an order to show
cause why the penalties established in Title 78B, Chapter 6, Part 3, Contempt,
should not be imposed.
