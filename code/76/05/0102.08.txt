76-5-102.8.   Disarming a peace officer -- Penalties.
(1)  As used in this section:
(a)  "Conductive energy device" means a weapon that uses electrical current to
disrupt voluntary control of muscles.
(b)  "Firearm" has the same meaning as in Section 76-10-501.
(2) An actor is guilty of an offense under Subsection (3) who intentionally
takes or removes, or attempts to take or remove a firearm or a conductive
energy device from the person or immediate presence of a person the actor
knows is a peace officer:
(a)  without the consent of the peace officer; and
(b)  while the peace officer is acting within the scope of his authority as a
peace officer.
(3) (a)  Conduct under Subsection (2) regarding a firearm is a first degree
felony.
(b)  Conduct under Subsection (2) regarding a conductive energy device is a
third degree felony.
