76-5-409.   Corroboration of admission by child's statement.
(1)  Notwithstanding any provision of law requiring corroboration of
admissions or confessions, and notwithstanding any prohibition of hearsay
evidence, a child's statement indicating in any manner the occurrence of the
sexual offense involving the child is sufficient corroboration of the
admission or the confession regardless of whether or not the child is
available to testify regarding the offense.
(2)  A child, for purposes of Subsection (1), is a person under the age of 14.
