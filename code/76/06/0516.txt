76-6-516.   Conveyance of real estate by married man without wife's consent.
Any married man who falsely represents himself as unmarried and under such
representation knowingly conveys or mortgages real estate situate in this
state, without the assent or concurrence of his wife when such consent or
concurrence is necessary to relinquish her inchoate statutory interest
therein, is guilty of a felony of the third degree.
