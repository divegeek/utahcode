76-6-504.   Tampering with records -- Penalty.
(1)  Any person who, having no privilege to do so, knowingly falsifies,
destroys, removes, or conceals any writing, other than the writings enumerated
in Section 76-6-503.5 for which the law provides public recording or any
record, public or private, with intent to deceive or injure any person or to
conceal any wrongdoing is guilty of tampering with records.
(2)  Tampering with records is a class B misdemeanor.
