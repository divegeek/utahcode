76-10-1505.   Discharging firearms and hurling missiles into buses and
terminals -- Exception.
(1)  Any person who discharges a firearm or hurls a missile at or into any bus
or terminal shall be guilty of a third degree felony.
(2)  The prohibition of this section does not apply to elected or appointed
peace officers or commercial security personnel who discharge firearms or hurl
missiles in the course and scope of their employment.
