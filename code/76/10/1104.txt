76-10-1104.   Gambling promotion.
(1)  A person is guilty of gambling promotion if he derives or intends to
derive an economic benefit other than personal winnings from gambling and:
(a)  he induces or aids another to engage in gambling; or
(b)  he knowingly invests in, finances, owns, controls, supervises, manages,
or participates in any gambling.
(2)  Gambling promotion is a class B misdemeanor, provided, however that any
person who is twice convicted under this section shall be guilty of a felony
of the third degree.
