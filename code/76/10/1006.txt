76-10-1006.   Selling or dealing with articles bearing registered trademark or service mark with intent to defraud.

Every person who, without the consent of the owner of an article bearing the owner's validly registered trademark or service mark, knowingly sells or traffics in the articles or who withholds the articles from the owner thereof with intent to defraud the owner thereof is guilty of a class B misdemeanor.



Enacted by Chapter 196, 1973 General Session
