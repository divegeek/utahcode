76-10-1103.   Gambling fraud.
(1) A person is guilty of gambling fraud if he participates in gambling and
wins or acquires to himself or another any gambling proceeds when he knows he
has a lesser risk of losing or greater chance of winning than one or more of
the other participants, and the risk is not known to all participants.
(2)  A person convicted of gambling fraud shall be punished as in the case of
theft of property of like value.
