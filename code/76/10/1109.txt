76-10-1109.   Confidence game -- Punishment as for theft -- Description in
charge.
(1) Any person who obtains or attempts to obtain from any other person any
money or property by any means, instrument or device commonly called a
confidence game shall be punished as in the case of theft of property of like
value.
(2)  In every indictment, information, or complaint under this section, it
shall be deemed and held a sufficient description of the offense to charge
that the accused did, on, ____ (insert the date) unlawfully and knowingly
obtain or attempt to obtain (as the case may be) from ____, (insert the name
of the person or persons defrauded or attempted to be defrauded) his money or
property (as the case may be) by means and by use of a confidence game.
