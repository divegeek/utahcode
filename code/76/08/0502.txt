76-8-502.   False or inconsistent material statements.
A person is guilty of a felony of the second degree if in any official
proceeding:
(1)  He makes a false material statement under oath or affirmation or swears
or affirms the truth of a material statement previously made and he does not
believe the statement to be true; or
(2)  He makes inconsistent material statements under oath or affirmation, both
within the period of limitations, one of which is false and not believed by
him to be true.
