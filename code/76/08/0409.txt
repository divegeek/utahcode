76-8-409.   Refusing to give tax assessor or tax or license collector list
of, or denying access to, employees.
Every person who, when requested by the assessor or collector of taxes or
license fees, refuses to give to the assessor or collector the name and
residence of each person in his employ, or to give the assessor or collector
access to the building or place of employment, is guilty of a class B
misdemeanor.
