76-8-717.   Violations -- Classifications of offenses.

Except as otherwise provided, a person found guilty of a violation of this part shall be punished as follows:

(1)  upon the first and second conviction, the person is guilty of a class B misdemeanor; or

(2)  if the person has previously been convicted two or more times of a violation of this part, the person is guilty of a class A misdemeanor.
