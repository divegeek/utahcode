76-8-407.   Refusing to give tax assessment information, or giving false
information.
Every person who unlawfully refuses, upon demand, to give to any county
assessor or deputy county assessor a list of his property subject to taxation,
or to swear to such list, or who gives a false name, or fraudulently refuses
to give his true name when demanded by the assessor in the discharge of his
official duties, is guilty of a class B misdemeanor.
