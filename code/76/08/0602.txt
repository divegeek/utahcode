76-8-602.   Assuming liability for conferring jurisdiction upon justice
court judge.
Any person who binds himself, or voluntarily becomes liable jointly or jointly
and severally with any other person, for the purpose of conferring
jurisdiction of any cause upon any justice court judge in any precinct or city
that would be without jurisdiction except for the liability of the joint
obligor, and any person who induces a person to assume the liability for the
purpose of conferring jurisdiction upon the justice court judge, is guilty of
a class B misdemeanor.
