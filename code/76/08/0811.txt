76-8-811.   Bargaining rights of employees not impaired by sabotage
prevention laws.
Nothing in this part shall be construed to impair, curtail, or destroy the
rights of employees and their representatives to self organize, to form, join,
or assist labor organizations, to bargain collectively through representatives
of their own choosing, and to engage in concerted activities, for the purpose
of collective bargaining or other mutual aid or protection as provided by
state or federal laws.
